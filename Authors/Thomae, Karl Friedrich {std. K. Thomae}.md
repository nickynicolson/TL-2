### Author

Thomae, Karl Friedrich (1863-?), German (Hessen-Nassau) botanist; studied at Leipzig University, student of J.A. Schenk; Dr. phil. ib. 1886. (*K. Thomae*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 374; CSP 19: 82.

#### Biofile

- Thomae, K.F., Blattst. Farne \[67, vita\] 1886 (b. 12 Mai 1863, son of Jakob Thomae of Flacht, distr. Wiesbaden).
