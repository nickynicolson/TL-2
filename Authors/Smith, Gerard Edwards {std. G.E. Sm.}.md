### Author

Smith, Gerard Edwards (1804-1881), British clergyman and botanist; BA Oxon. 1829; curate at Sellinge (Kent) 1829-1830; vicar at Cantley (Yorks.) 1844-1846; id. at Osmaston (Derby) 1854-1871. (*G.E. Sm.*).
