### Author

Nemoto, Kwanji (1860-1936), Japanese botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Nemoto*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – For type specimens at [[Collection TO|TO]] see his list in Nat. Sci. & Mus. 5(5): 15-14 \[sic\]. 1934.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 545; BM 7; 909; Bossert p. 284; Hortus 3: 1200 ("Nemoto"); IF suppl. 4: 334; Kew 4: 129; MW p. 353, suppl. p. 256.

### Publications

##### n.6741. Flora of Japan supplement

**Title**
Nippon-shokubutsu-soran-hoi (*Flora of Japan supplement*). The enumeration of all the plants phanerogams and higher cryptogams indigenous to, introduced into and cultivated in the empire of Japan Kabafuto, Hokkaido, Honshiu, Shikoku, Kiushiu, Riukiu, and Taiwan with the description of every plant published after the second edition... Tokyo (Shunyodo Shoten...) 1936. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Fl. Japan, Suppl.*

**Notes**
