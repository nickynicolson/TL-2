### Author

Duncker, Bernardus Johannes Josephus (fl. 1935), born at Amsterdam, studied at Utrecht with F.A.F.C. Went, Dr. in 1935. 
**Abbreviated name**: *B.J.J. Duncker*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
