### Author

Tafalla, Juan José (1755(?)-1811), Spanish botanist; pharmacist in Navarra; with Ruiz and Pavon on their expedition to Peru; professor of botany at Lima from 1788. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tafalla*

#### Herbarium and types

See H. Ruiz and J. Pavon; main collections (in those of Ruiz and Pavon) at [[Collection MA|MA]]; some material at [[Collection B|B]]-Willd., [[Collection FI|FI]], [[Collection K|K]].
