### Author

Naumburg, Samuel Johann (1768-1799), German botanist; professor of botany at Erfurt (*Naumb.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1404; CSP 4: 579; Dryander 3: 389; PR 6619-6620 (ed. 1: 7389-7390); Rehder 1: 84.

#### Eponymy

*Naumburgia* Moench (1802).
