### Author

Wilke, Fritz \[Wilhelm\] (1888-x), German (Prussian/Westphalian) botanist; studied natural sciences at Jena, Berlin and Halle; Dr. phil. Halle 1913. (*Wilke*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

We have no information on Hermann Wilke (from Königsberg, Preussen), author of *Die Phylogenie der Rhodophyceae*, Leipzig (Akademische Verlagsgesellschaft...) 1929, cover-t.p., p. \[1\]-85, repr. from Bot. Arch. 26(1/2). 1929, and *Ein auf die Auxiliarzellen begründetes Florideen-System*, id. 24(3-4): \[415\]-423. 1929. *Copies* of reprints at B.
