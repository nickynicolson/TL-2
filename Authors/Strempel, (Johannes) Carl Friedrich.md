### Author

Strempel, \[Johannes\] Carl Friedrich (1800-1872), German (Mecklenburg) physician and botanist; studied in Rostock and (1818-1822) Berlin; Dr. phil. Berlin 1822; practicing physician in Schwerin ca. 1822-1825; from 1825 physician (from 1838 as "Ober-Medizinalrath") in Rostock. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Strempel*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – *Letters* to D.F.L. von Schlechtendal at [[Collection HAL|HAL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

ADB 36: 573-575 (Ad. Hofmeister); Barnhart 3: 339 (b. 20 Aug 1800); BM 5: 2035; Herder p. 271; IF p. 732; PR 9004, ed. 1: 9957; Zander ed. 10, p. 722, ed. 11, p. 823.

#### Biofile

- Blanck, A., Die mecklenb. Ärzte 151 f. 1874 (n.v.).
- Meyer, D.E., Nova Hedwigia 35: 603-637. 1981 (on Strempel's dissertation and on *Botrychium matricariifolium*).

#### Dedication

Roeper, J., *Botanische Thesen*, Rostock 1872, dedicated to Strempel to commemorate the 50th anniversary of the publication of his thesis.

#### Eponymy

*Strempelia* A. Richard ex A.P. de Candolle (1830) and possibly the derived *Slrempeliopsis* Bentham (1876).
