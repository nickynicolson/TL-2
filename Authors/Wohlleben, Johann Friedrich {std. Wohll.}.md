### Author

Wohlleben, Johann Friedrich (x-1796), German (Westphalen) botanist; physician in Halle; Dr. med. Halle 1796. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Wohll.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection REG|REG]] (Lich.).

#### Bibliography and biography
