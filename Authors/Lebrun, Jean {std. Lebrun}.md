### Author

Lebrun, Jean (1906-x), Belgian botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lebrun*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]]; for duplicates see IH.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 356; BFM 1684; BL 1: 25, 305, 2: 32,680; IH 2: 421; Kew 3: 391.

#### Eponymy

Lebrunia Staner(1934); *Lebruniodendron Léonard* (1951).
