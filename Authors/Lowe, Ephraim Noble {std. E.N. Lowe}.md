### Author

Lowe, Ephraim Noble (1864-1933), American geologist and botanist; director of the State Geological Survey of Mississippi (*E. N. Lowe*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MISS|MISS]]; further material [[Collection UN|UN]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 406 (b. 5 Mai 1864, d. 12 Sep 1933); BL 1: 193, 306; IH 1 (ed. 6): 361, 2: 464; Pennell p. 612.
