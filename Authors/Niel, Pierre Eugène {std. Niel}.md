### Author

Niel, Pierre Eugène (1836-1905), French banker, philanthropist and amateur botanist at Rouen. (*Niel*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Contributed specimens to C. Roumeguère, *Fungi exsiccati praecipue gallici* (e.g. to cent. 60-69).
