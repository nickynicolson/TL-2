### Author

Salter, Thomas Bell (1814-1858), Scottish physician and botanist; Dr. med. Edinburgh; practiced at Ryde (Isle of Wight). 
**Abbreviated name**: *T.B. Salter*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]]; further material at [[Collection AK|AK]], [[Collection CGE|CGE]], [[Collection E|E]], Ryde Museum.

Barnhart 3: 204; BB p. 267; BL 2: 238, 243, 713; BM 4: 1794; CSP 5: 385; Desmond p. 539-540 (d. 30 Sep 1858); Jackson p. 258; Kew 4: 580; PR 8019, ed. 1: 8976; Rehder 5: 756; TL-2/792; Tucker 1: 621.
