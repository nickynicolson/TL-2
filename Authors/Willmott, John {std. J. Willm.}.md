### Author

Willmott, John (1775-1834), British commercial horticulturist at Lewisham nursery. (*J. Willm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Desmond p. 665; Henrey 2: 386, 3: 136; Rehder 1: 522; Tucker 1: 749.
