### Author

Wahlström, Johan Erik (1821-1892), Swedish botanist and high school teacher; studied in Uppsala 1842-1848; Dr. phil. 1848; teacher at Stockholm 1856-1888. (*Wahlström*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 449; BL 2: 549, 729; BM 5: 2250; KR p. 747 (b. 12 Apr 1821, d. 22 Feb 1892); PR 9921; Rehder 1: 355; TL-2/see J.A. Schagerström.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Brit. Mus. gen. cat. print. books 251: 598. 1965.
