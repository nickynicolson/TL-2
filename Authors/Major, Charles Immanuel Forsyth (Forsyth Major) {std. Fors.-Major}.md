### Author

Major, Charles Immanuel Forsyth \[Forsyth Major\] (1843-1923), British-Swiss botanist and palaeontologist; practicing physician 1868-1886; working on the mediterranean and madagascan floras; MD Basel 1868. (*Major*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BAS|BAS]] (Stehlin 1925, p. 15), [[Collection BM|BM]] (Madagascar), [[Collection K|K]] (Corsica); further material et [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection FI|FI]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection LY|LY]], [[Collection P|P]] and [[Collection TO|TO]].
