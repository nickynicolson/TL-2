### Author

Eberwein, Richard (fl. 1904), Austrian botanist, professor in Staab (Bohemia), specialist in moorland investigation. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Eberwein*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
