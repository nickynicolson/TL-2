### Author

Sadler, Michael (*fl*. 1831), Hungarian botanist; brother of Joseph Sadler. (*M. Sadler*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some of the material listed under Joseph Sadler may have been collected by Michael Sadler.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 198; Futak-Domin p. 517; IH 1 (ed. 7): 341, 2: (files); Kanitz p. 104; PR 7962, ed. 1: 8916; Rehder 2: 102; Tucker 1: 617.
