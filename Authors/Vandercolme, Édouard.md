### Author

Vandercolme, Édouard (*fl*. 1870), French physician; studied at the Faculté de Médecine, Paris; Dr. med. ib. 1870. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Vandercolme*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
