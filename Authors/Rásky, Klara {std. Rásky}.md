### Author

Rásky, Klara (1908-1971), Hungarian paleobotanist at the Department of Botany of the Museum of Natural History, Budapest 1938-1971; specialist on fossil of Characeae. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rásky*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BP|BP]].
