### Author

Micheletti, Luigi (1844-1912), Italian army officer, lichenologist and general floristic botanist. (*Micheletti*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TO|TO]]; further material [[Collection PAD|PAD]] (types), [[Collection FI|FI]] (Erytrea).
