### Author

Thorold, Charles Aubrey (1906-x), British mycologist; MA Oxford (Trinity) 1934; mycologist with the Kenya Department of Agriculture from 1930, later stationed at Trinidad. (*Thorold*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection EA|EA]]; lichens e.g., at [[Collection M|M]] and [[Collection UPS|UPS]]; further material at [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection BM|BM]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 381 (b. 9 Nov 1906); GR p. 383.
