### Author

Pillans, Neville Stuart (1884-1964), South African botanist; studied at Cambridge; at the Bolus Herbarium 1918-1953. (*Pillans*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BOL|BOL]] and [[Collection NBG|NBG]]; further material at [[Collection BH|BH]], [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection J|J]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection MO|MO]], [[Collection PRE|PRE]], [[Collection SAM|SAM]], [[Collection STE|STE]], [[Collection U|U]].
