### Author

Quartin-Dillon, Richard (x-1841), French botanist, explorer of Abyssinia (1839-1840). (*Quart.-Dill.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]] and [[Collection PC|PC]], duplicates e.g. in [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection CN|CN]], [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection LE|LE]], [[Collection MO|MO]], [[Collection W|W]].

#### Composite works

(1) A. Richard, *Tentamen florae Abyssinicae* 1847, q.v., is based on the collections made by R. Quartin-Dillon & A. Petit, 1838-1843. This is part 3, torn. 4-5 of *Voyage en Abyssinie*, exécuté pendant... 1839-1843, 6 vols., atlas, 1845-1851.

#### Eponymy
