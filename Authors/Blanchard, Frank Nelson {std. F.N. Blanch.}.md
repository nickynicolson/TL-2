### Author

Blanchard, Frank Nelson (1888-1937), American (Mass.) zoologist, studied at Tufts College, Medford nr. Boston, Mass., A.B. 1913 ib., at the Agricultural College, Amherst, Mass. from 1913, Ph.D. University of Michigan 1919. (*F.N. Blanch.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 197 (b. 19 Dec 1888, d. 21 Sep 1937); TL-2/5289.
