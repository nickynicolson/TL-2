### Author

Townson, William (1850-1926), British-born New Zealand pharmaceutical chemist and plant collector. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Townson*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 395; BL 1: 85, 321; Desmond p. 615 (d. 11 Aug 1926).

#### Biofile

- Aston, B.C., Trans. Proc. New Zealand Inst. 58(1-2): 186-188. 1927 (obit., portr.).
- Cheeseman, T.F., Manual New Zealand Fl. xxx, xxxiv. 1906.

#### Note

(1) Published *On the vegetation of the Westport district*, Trans. Proc. New Zealand Inst. 39: 380-433, *pl. 14, 14a.* Jun 1907. See BL 1: 85.
(2) Robert Townson published *Travels in Hungary* with a short account of Vienna in the year 1793... London (printed for G.G. and J. Robinson,...) 1797. Qu. (*Trav. Hungary*), which has a list of plants (some with descriptions) on p. 477-494. Also in French: *Voyage en Hongrie*, Paris, an vii, 3 vols. (repr. An xi, 1803).

#### Eponymy

*Townsonia* Cheeseman (1906).
