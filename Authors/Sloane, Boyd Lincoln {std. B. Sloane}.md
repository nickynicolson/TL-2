### Author

Sloane, Boyd Lincoln (1885-?), South African botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *B. Sloane*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 287; Hortus 3: 1204; NI 3: 68; Zander ed. 10, p. 717, ed. 11, p. 818.

#### Composite works

(1) With Alain White (q.v.). *The Stapelieae* 1933, ed. 2. 1937.
(2) With Alain White (q.v.). *The succulent Euphorbieae of South Africa*. 1941.
