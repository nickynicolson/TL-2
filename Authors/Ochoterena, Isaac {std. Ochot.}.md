### Author

Ochoterena, Isaac (1885-1950), Mexican botanist, director of the botanical section of the Directión Estud. Biol. Mex. from 1915. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ochot.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MEXU|MEXU]].
