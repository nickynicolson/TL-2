### Author

Edgren, Peter Adolph (1902-1891), Swedish botanist, born 7 Feb 1802 at Åmål, student at Uppsala 1829, med. dr. 1835, in military service 1868-1870, died 3 Jul 1891 at Stockholm. (*Edgren*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
