### Author

Turnbull, Robert (c. 1813-1891), British naturalist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Turnbull*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; *letters* to W.F. Farlow at [[Collection FH|FH]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 409; BB p. 306; BL 2: 224, 460, 725; Desmond p. 621 (d. 19 Jan 1891); Rehder 5: 865.

### Publications

##### n.15.359. Index of British plants

**Title**
*Index of British plants* according to the London Catalogue (eighth edition). Including the synonyms used by the principal authors, an alphabetical list of English names, also references to the illustrations of Syme's English botany, and Bentham's British flora... London (George Bell and Sons,...) 1889. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Index Brit. pl.*
