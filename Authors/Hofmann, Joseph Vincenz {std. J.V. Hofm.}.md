### Author

Hofmann, Joseph Vincenz (1800-1863), Austrian clergyman ("Consistorialrath") and botanist at Brixen. (*J. V. Hofm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection IBF|IBF]]; further material [[Collection LZ|LZ]].
