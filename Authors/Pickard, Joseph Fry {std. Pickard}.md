### Author

Pickard, Joseph Fry (1876-1943), British draper, tea merchant and botanist in Yorkshire (Leeds). (*Pickard*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BM|BM]] and [[Collection LES|LES]]; further material at [[Collection LIV|LIV]].

- Kent, D.H., Brit. herb. 73. 1957.
