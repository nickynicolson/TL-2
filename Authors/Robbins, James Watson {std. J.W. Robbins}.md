### Author

Robbins, James Watson (1801-1879), American (New England) botanist; Dr. med. Yale 1828; practicing physician at Uxbridge, Mass. 1830-1869, surgeon and physician with a copper-mining company nr Portage Lake, Lake Superior 1839-1863; travelling in Cuba and Texas 1863-1864; from 1864-1879 again in Uxbridge devoting himself to botany. (*J.W. Robbins*).
