### Author

Notø, Andreas (1865-1948), Norwegian botanist; high school teacher at Tromsø (1888-1909), Levanger (1909-1920) and Bodø (from 1920): (*Notø*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TROM|TROM]]; further material at [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection H|H]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection TRH|TRH]].
