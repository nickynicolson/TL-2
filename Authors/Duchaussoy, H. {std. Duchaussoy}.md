### Author

Duchaussoy, H. (fl. 1896), French botanist, member of Société Linn. Nord. France from 1888. (*Duchaussoy*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 475; BL 2: 139, 205, 648; LS 32.756; Rehder 1: 535, Tucker 1: 213.
