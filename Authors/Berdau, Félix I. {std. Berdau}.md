### Author

Berdau, Félix I. (1826-1895), Polish physician and botanist at Krakow, later at Warsawa. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Berdau*

#### HERBARIUM and TYPES

KRA, KRAM, WA.
*Ref*.: GR p. 723; IH 2: 69.
