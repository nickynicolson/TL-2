### Author

Collins, Marjorie Isabel (fl. 1918-1921), Australian botanist, B.Sc., Sydney, Demonstrator in Botany at the University of Adelaide. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M.I. Collins*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
