### Author

\* Cruse, \[Carl Friedrich\] Wilhelm (1803-1873), German (born in Mitau (Latvia)) physician and botanist, professor in the University of Königsberg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Cruse*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection KBG|KBG]].

#### Bibliography and biography
