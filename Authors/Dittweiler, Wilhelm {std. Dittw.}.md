### Author

Dittweiler, Wilhelm (fl. 1847), German veterinarian, teacher at the veterinary school in Karlsruhe. (*Dittw.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 2: 465; PR: 2334, ed. 1: 2644; Rehder 1: 88.
