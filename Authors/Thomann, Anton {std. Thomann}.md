### Author

Thomann, Anton (*fl*. 1859), Austrian high school teacher and botanist at the Gymnasium of Krems. (*Thomann*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2095; PR 9219; Rehder 1: 441.

**Abbreviated title**: *Syn. Krems Phan.*

**Notes**
