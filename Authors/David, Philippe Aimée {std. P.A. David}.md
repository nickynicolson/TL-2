### Author

David, Philippe Aimée (fl. 1878), French physician and botanist, Chevalier de la Légion-d'Honneur. (*P.A. David*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 137-138, 645; Jackson p. 281; Rehder 1: 415.
