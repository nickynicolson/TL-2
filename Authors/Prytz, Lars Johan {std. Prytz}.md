### Author

Prytz, Lars Johan (1789-1823), Finnish botanist. (*Prytz*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection H|H]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 114; BM 4: 1620; CSP 5: 37; Herder p. 200; IH 2: (in press); PR 7356 (ed. 1: 8243); Rehder 5: 693; Saelan p. 399; TL-2/2610; TR 1093; Tucker 1: 572.
