### Author

Mousnier, Jules (*fl*. 1873), French pharmacist born at Saujon (Charente-Inférieure); Dr. pharm. Paris 1873. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mousnier*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
