### Author

Náves, Andres (1839-1910), Spanish Augustinian friar; collected in the Philippine Islands 
**Abbreviated name**: *Náves*

#### Herbarium and types

Original collections destroyed; some material at [[Collection MA|MA]].

#### Composite works

Editor (and co-author of Noviss. App.) of Blanco, *Fl. Filip., ed. 9, q.v.* (TL-2/553). For a review of the *Noviss App*. see for instance Peter, Bot. Centralbl. 18: 175-176. 1884.
