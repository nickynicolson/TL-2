### Author

Monnard, Jean Pierre (1791-?), Swiss botanist and high school teacher at Nyon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Monnard*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 505; CSP 4: 442; Hortus 3: 1200; PR (alph.) (ed. 1: 7064), TL-2/1971-1972.

#### Composite works

Edited Gaudin, *Syn. fl. Helv.*, 1836, see TL-2/1972.
