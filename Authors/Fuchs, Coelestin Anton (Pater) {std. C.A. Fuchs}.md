### Author

Fuchs, Coelestin Anton (Pater) (fl. 1898-1909), Austrian botanist and priest, high school teacher Komotau, "Böhmen" 1909, at least. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C.A. Fuchs*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
