### Author

Dye, Clair Albert (fl. 1899-1901), American born botanist who studied at the pharmaceutical institute at Bern, Switzerland. (*Dye*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Biography and bibliography

Kew 2: 157.
