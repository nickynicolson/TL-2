### Author

Wuensche, Johann Georg (*fl*. 1804), German (Prussian-Saxony) physician and botanist at Wittenberg. (*J. Wuensche*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2366; PR 10413, ed. 1: 11367; Rehder 1: 375.
