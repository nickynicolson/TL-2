### Author

Pluskal (Pluska-Moravicanský), Franiscek Sal. (1811-1901), Moravian botanist; practicing physician at Lomnitz 1839-1857; later in Welechrad. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Pluskal*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BRNU|BRNU]].
