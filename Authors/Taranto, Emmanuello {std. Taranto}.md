### Author

Taranto, Emmanuello (*fl*. 1845), Italian natural scientist; professor at the Cabinet of natural history and archeology of Calatogirone. (*Taranto*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 1: 396, 722; Jackson p. 322; LS 26300.
