### Author

Mendonça, Francisco de Ascensão (1889-?), Portuguese botanist; specialist on Capparidaceae and Malvaceae; travelled in Angola 1927, 1937, 1955, in Moçambique 1942-1948. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mendonça*
