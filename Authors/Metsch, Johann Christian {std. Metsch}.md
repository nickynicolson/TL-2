### Author

Metsch, Johann Christian (1796-1856), German botanist; Dr. med. Berlin 1821; from 1822 public health officer at Suhl. (*Metsch*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection JE|JE]]; a collection of *Rubus* was acquired by B in 1895.
