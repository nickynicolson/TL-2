### Author

Trinchinetti, Augusto (1813-1847), Italian botanist; Dr. med. Pavia 1835, later sometime professor of botany in Pavia. (*Trinchinetti*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 401 (b. 28 Mar 1813, d. 12 Aug 1847); BM 5: 2140; Bossert p. 406; CSP 6: 42; Herder p. 124; LS 27179; PR 6442, 9510, ed. 1: 7171, 7226, 10475; Rehder 1: 161, 182; Saccardo 1: 166, 229-231.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Brit. Mus. gen. cat. print. books 241: 444. 1964.
- Candolle, A.P. de, Mém. souv. 482. 1862.

#### Eponymy

*Trinchinettia* Endlicher (1841) very likely honors this author.
