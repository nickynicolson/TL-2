### Author

Widnmann, Friedrich (1765-1848), German (Bavarian) botanist and physician at Eichstädt. (*Widnmann*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2315; Herder p. 159; Jackson p. 426; PR 745, 10222, ed. 1: 840, 11205; SO add. 7859.
