### Author

Lendner, Alfred (1873-1948), Swiss mycologist. (*Lendn.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 368; BL 2: 571, 681; BM 7: 620; CSP 16: 707; Hawksworth p. 184; Hirsch p. 172; Kew 3: 410; LS 15207-15213, 35938-35945, suppl. 15809-15843; Stevenson p. 1249; Tucker 1: 422.

#### Eponymy

*Lendneria* Minod (1918).
