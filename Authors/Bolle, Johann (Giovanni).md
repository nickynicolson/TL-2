### Author

Bolle, Johann \[Giovanni\] (1850-?), Triest born viniculturist and agriculturist at the Experiment Station for viniculture at Gorizia (Görz, Goritz) in Slovenia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Bolle*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
