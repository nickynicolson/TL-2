### Author

Behrendsen, \[August Wilhelm\] Otto (fl. 1876), German botanist, geologist and palaeobotanist, sometime teacher at the Gymnasium in Hildesheim. 
**Abbreviated name**: *O. Behrendsen*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
