### Author

Ulvinen, \[Eero\] Arvi (1897-x), Finnish botanist; high school teacher at Kouvola and Helsinki. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ulvinen*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection H|H]] (incl. coll. [[Collection KYM|KYM]]).
