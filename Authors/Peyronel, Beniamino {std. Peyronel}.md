### Author

Peyronel, Beniamino (1890-x), Italian mycologist at the Istituto botanico dell'Università, Torina 
**Abbreviated name**: *Peyronel*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection ROPV|ROPV]].– Some letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Note

published as late as 1971: Inform. bot. Ital. 2(3): 163-167. 1970 (1971 : on conservation of nature).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 77; BL 2: 200, 703; Hirsch p. 231; IH 1(ed. 1): 94, (ed. 2): 19, (ed. 3): 152, (ed. 4): 170, (ed. 5): 184, (ed. 6): 273, 2: (in press); Kelly p. 175; Kew 4: 289; LS 21584-21627 (bibl.); Stevenson p. 1254.

#### Biofile

- Ciferri, R., Taxon 1(8): 127. 1952 (types at ROPV).
- Verdoorn, F., ed., Chron. bot. 3: 189. 1937, 4: 273. 1938.

#### Eponymy

*Peyronelia* R. Ciferri et R.G. Fragoso (1927); *Peyronellula* Malan (1953); *Note*: *Peyronelina* P. J. Fisher, J. Webster et D.F. Kane (1927) and *Peyronellaea* G. Goidanich (1952) were probably also named for Peyronel.
