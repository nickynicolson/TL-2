### Author

Rainer, Moriz (Moritz) von und zu Haarbach (1793-1847), Austrian botanist; for eighteen years banker in Milano; ultimately at Graz. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rainer*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection GJO|GJO]] (rd. 1851); further material in [[Collection BR|BR]] (Mart.), [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection M|M]], [[Collection LZ|LZ]] and [[Collection REG|REG]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 6(2): 744-745; Barnhart 3: 124; DTS 1: 233, xxiii; IH 2: (in press); Lasègue p. 336, 337; Saccardo 1: 135.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Flora 30: 542. 1847 (d.); Österr. bot. W. 1: 12. 1851 (herbarium and library left to GJO).
- Hausmann, F. v., Fl. Tirol. 3: 1184. 1854.
- Müller, R.H.W. & R. Zaunick, Friedrich Traugott Kützing 136. 1960.

#### Eponymy

*Rainera* G. de Notaris (1838); *Campanula raineri* Lena-Perpenti does not commemorate M. Rainer but rather the Archduke Rainer (1783-1853) at the time viceroy of Lombardy and Venice (AG 6(2): 644-645).
