### Author

Strutt, Jacob George (*fl*. 1820-1850), British landscape painter and etcher; at Lausanne and Rome 1830-1851. (*Strutt*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 341; BB p. 292; BM 5: 2038; DNB 55: 64 (19: 64); Herder p. 382; Jackson p. 245; NI 1906-1908; Plesch p. 418; PR 9016, ed. 1 : 6289, 9971; Rehder 1: 296, 348, 516; Sotheby 723; Tucker 1: 671, 3: 297.
