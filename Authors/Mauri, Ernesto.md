### Author

Mauri, Ernesto (1791-1836), Italian botanist at Rome; professor of botany and director of the University Botanical Garden 1820-1831. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Mauri*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection RO|RO]]; further material at [[Collection FI|FI]].

#### Eponymy

*Mauria* Kunth (1824). *Note*: The derivation of *Maurinia* Niessl (1876) is not explained by Niessl.
