### Author

Puydt, Emile de (1810-1891), Belgian horticulturist at Mons. (*Puydt*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1625; CSP 8: 676; Herder p. 376; Jackson p. 138; Morren ed. 10, p. 46; MW p. 397; NI 1572 (b. 6 Mar 1810, d. 20 Mai 1891); Plesch p. 370; Rehder 5: 605; Sotheby no. 618.
