### Author

Suffren, François Palamède, Marquis de (1753-1824), French botanist at Salon, Bouches-du-Rhône. (*Suffren*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection P|P]]-JU and [[Collection H|H]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 345 (b. 27 Aug 1753, d. 27 Aug 1824); BM 5: 2046; CSP 5: 884; De Toni 1: cxxi; GR p. 539; Herder p. 96, 193; Lasègue 342; LS 26143; PR 9041, ed. 1: 9993; Rehder 1: 425; Saccardo 1: 159, 2: 104-105, cron. p. xxxiii; SO 778.

#### Biofile

- Amoreux, Mém. Soc. Linn. Paris 1: 718-719. 1822 (corr. with A. Gouan).
- Candolle, A.P. de, Mém. souv. 227. 1862.
- Reynier, A., Rev. mens. Soc. Hort. Bot. Marseille 40: 195-202. 1894 (correspondence).

#### Eponymy

*Suffrenia* Bellardi (1803-1804).
