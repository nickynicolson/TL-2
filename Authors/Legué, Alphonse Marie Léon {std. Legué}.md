### Author

Legué, Alphonse Marie Léon (1841-1920), French botanist at Mondoubleau in Loir-et Cher. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Legué*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]], [[Collection PC|PC]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 3: 764; Barnhart 2: 362; BL 2: 171, 680; CSP 16: 683; IH 2: 426; LS 15114-15115; Rehder 5: 499; Tucker 1: 420.
- Anon., Bull. Soc. bot. Deux-Sèvres 21: 322, *pl. 2.* 1910 (portr., signature).
- Bois, Bull. Soc. bot. France 67: 398. 1920.
