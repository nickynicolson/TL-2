### Author

Elst, Paulus von der (1883-x), Dutch botanist, born 1883 at Schiedam, studied at the University of Utrecht, Ph.D. 1909. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Elst*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
