### Author

Smith, Henry (*fl*. 1810), British physician and botanist at Salisbury infirmary. (*He. Sm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 293; BM 4: 1946; Desmond p. 567; Jackson p. 259; NI 1856; Plesch p. 409; PR 8732; Rehder 1: 396; Sotheby 705; Tucker 1: 655.
