### Author

Tarrade, Adrien (1843-1889), French botanist (mycologist) and pharmacist in the Limoges area. (*Tarrade*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 360 (d. 27 Apr 1889); BM 5: 2073; Jackson p. 286; LS 26309.
