### Author

Clarion, Jacques (1776-1844), French physician, phytochemist and army pharmacist. (*Clarion*).

#### Herbarium and types

some material in [[Collection P|P]]-JU.
*Ref*.: IH 2: 128 (as "Jean," probably erroneous).

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 2(1): 203-204; Barnhart 1: 350 (b. 1776); BM 1: 351; Bossert p. 75 (b. 1779); PR 1729.
- Candolle, Mémoires et souvenirs 139. 1862.
- Chatin, Bull. Soc. bot. France 38(1): 89-93. 1891.
- Wittrock, Acta Horti Berg. 3(3): 99. 1905 (b. 1779).
- Balland, Les pharmaciens militaires français 279. 1913 (b. 12 Oct 1779).

#### Composite works

Palisot de Beauvois, *Essai* 1812 (p. 205: *Deyeuxia*). Contributed also to Candolle, *Fl. franç.* ed. 3 (1805-1815).

#### Eponymy

*Clarionea* Lagasca ex A. P. de Candolle (1812).
