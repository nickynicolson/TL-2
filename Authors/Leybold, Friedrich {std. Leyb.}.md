### Author

Leybold, Friedrich (1827-1879), German botanist and pharmacist; pupil of Martius; settled in Chile (1855) as pharmaceutical industrialist. (*Leyb.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
*Ref*.: Anon., Flora 37: 383, 416. 1854 (sale herb.)

### Publications

##### n.4480. Stirpium in alpibus orientali-australibus nuperrime repertarum

**Title**
*Stirpium in alpibus orientali-australibus nuperrime repertarum* non nullarumquc non satis adhuc expositarum icones quibus brevem ex recentissimis observationibus derivatam adjunxit descriptionem auctor Fr. Leybold monocensis. Ratisbonae \[Regensburg\] (typis Neubauerianis) 1855. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Stirp. alp. repert.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 14 Jun 1855 (in Flora: p. \[3\]: Mar 1855), p. \[1\]-27, *pl. 1-12* (uncoloured (G, M) liths. of drawings by the author). *Copies*: G, M, Regensburg; the latter copy has exquisitely coloured lithographs. – Reprinted from Flora 38: 337-349, pl. 4-15. 14 Jun 1855.
