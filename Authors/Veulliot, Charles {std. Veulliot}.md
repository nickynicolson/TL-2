### Author

Veulliot, Charles (1829-1890), French tax inspector and amateur mycologist in Pointde-Vaux, from 1873 in Lyon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Veulliot*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
