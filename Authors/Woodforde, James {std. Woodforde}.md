### Author

Woodforde, James (1771-1837), British physician and botanist; MD Edinburgh 1825. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Woodforde*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 517; BB p. 334; BM 5: 2354; Desmond p. 674 (d. 6 Jul 1837); Jackson p. 252; Kelly p. 243; LS 29246; PR 10396, ed. 1: 11357; Rehder 1: 397; Tucker 1: 753.

#### Eponymy

*Woodfordia* R.A. Salisbury (1806), honors E.James Alexander Woodford (*fl*. 1790's), English gardener.
