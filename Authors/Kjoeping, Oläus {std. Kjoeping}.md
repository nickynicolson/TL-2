### Author

Kjoeping, Oläus (1741-1809), \[fictitious\] Swedish botanical explorer. (*Kjoeping*).

#### Name

Created by an unknown contributor to Appleton's Dictionary of American Biography (ca. 1890); said to have been a military surgeon in the Danish service. See Barnhart (1919) for further details. Fictitious titles: "*Prodromus florae*, sistens enumerationem plantarum cellularium quas in insulas, Santa Domingo, Cuba, Sancti Thomae et Sancti Bartholomei a Kjoeping collectas... Copenhagen 1799, and "*Anmärkingar om Planter of Cuba*" (3 vols. 1807). Reminiscent of Olov Swartz.
*Ref*.: Barnhart, J. H., J. New York Bot. Gard. 20: 174. 1919.
