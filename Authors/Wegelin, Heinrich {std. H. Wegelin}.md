### Author

Wegelin, Heinrich (1853-1940), Swiss botanist; professor of botany at the Kantonschule of Thurgau in Frauenfeld and director of its botanical garden 1890-1920. (*H. Wegelin*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. Some fungi from Switzerland in [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 470; BFM 995; BL 2: 582, 730; LS 28490-28491.

#### Biofile

- Gager, C.S., Brooklyn Bot. Gard. Rec. 27(3): 336. 1938.
