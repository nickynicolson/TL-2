### Author

Giordano, Ferdinando (fl. 1833-1842), Italian medical doctor in Naples (Kingdom of the Two Sicilies), associated with M. Tenore. (*Giord.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
