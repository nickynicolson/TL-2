### Author

Timkó, György (1881-1945), Hungarian lichenologist; in charge of the lichen collections of the Botanical Department of the Museum of Natural Sciences, Budapest. (*Timkó*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BP|BP]]. – Other material at [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection H|H]] (lich.), [[Collection LD|LD]] (lich.), [[Collection M|M]], [[Collection SOM|SOM]], [[Collection TUR|TUR]]-V, [[Collection UPS|UPS]].
