### Author

Rostock, Michael (1821-1893), German (Saxonian) botanist in the Oberlausitz. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rostock*

#### Herbarium and types

Some bryophytes in [[Collection MW|MW]]; further cryptogams at [[Collection GLM|GLM]].
