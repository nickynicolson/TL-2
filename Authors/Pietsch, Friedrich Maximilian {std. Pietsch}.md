### Author

Pietsch, Friedrich Maximilian (1856-?), German (Saxonian) botanist; Dr. phil. Halle 1893; high school teacher at Gera. (*Pietsch*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 86; BJI 1: 46; BM 4: 1576.
