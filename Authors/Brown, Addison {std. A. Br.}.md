### Author

Brown, Addison (1830-1913), American botanist, president of the New York Botanical Garden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Brown*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MASS|MASS]] (orig.), [[Collection NY|NY]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 100.
