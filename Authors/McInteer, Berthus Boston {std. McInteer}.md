### Author

McInteer, Berthus Boston (1887-1978), American algologist and ecologist in Kentukky; M. S. Univ. Kentucky 1926; Ph. D. Ohio State 1932; at the University of Kentucky as instructor in botany (1924-1925), asst. professor (1925-1933), assoc. prof. 1933-1957. (*McInteer*).
