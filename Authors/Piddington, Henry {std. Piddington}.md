### Author

Piddington, Henry (1797-1858), British meteorologist and botanist; commander in the mercantile marine; from circa 1800 curator of the Museum of economic geology and coroner at Calcutta. (*Piddington*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Piddington introduced (1848) the term and concept "cyclone" for disturbances (winds) with a whirling or circular course.
