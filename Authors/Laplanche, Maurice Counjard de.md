### Author

Laplanche, Maurice Counjard de (1843-1904), French mycologist and landed gentleman at Laplanche near Luzy (Nièvre) and at Souh-el-Khemis (Tunisia). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lapl.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection AUT|AUT]] (4000; mycol. herb.)
