### Author

Erlandsson, Stellan (1902-x), Swedish botanist, cytologist and diatomist, born 1902, Ph.D. at Uppsala in 1936. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Erl.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 513; Bossert p. 118; Kew 2: 204.
