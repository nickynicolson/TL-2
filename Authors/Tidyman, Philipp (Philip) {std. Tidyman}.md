### Author

Tidyman, Philipp \[Philip\] (x-1850), American botanist and palaeontologist of Charleston, S. Carolina; Dr. med. Göttingen 1800. (*Tidyman*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 383 (d. 11 Jul 1850); BM 5: 2113; Dawson p. 821; PR 9344, ed. 1: 10309.
