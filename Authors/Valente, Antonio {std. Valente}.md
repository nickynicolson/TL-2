### Author

Valente, Antonio (*fl*. 1803), Italian physician and botanist in Rome. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Valente*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2189; Jackson p. 437; LS 27602; PR 9659, ed. 1: 10624; Rehder 1: 61; Saccardo 1: 168; SO 783a.

#### Eponymy

*Valentiana* Rafinesque (1814) honors George Annesley, 2nd Earl of Mountnorris, Viscount Valentia (1769-1844), English botanist and traveller to India and the Middle East.
