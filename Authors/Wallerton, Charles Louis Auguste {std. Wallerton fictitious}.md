### Author

Wallerton, Charles Louis Auguste (1721-1788). Fictitious French naturalist, said to have explored the province of Michoacan, Mexico between 1760 and 1765 and to have written e.g. *Tableau de la flore du royaume de la Nouvelle Espagne* (2 vols. 1775-1779).
*Ref*.: Anon., in J.G. Wilson and J. Fiske, Appleton's Cycl. Amer. biogr. 6: 338. 1889 (wholly fictitious). (*==Name in the standard form is not specified==.*).
