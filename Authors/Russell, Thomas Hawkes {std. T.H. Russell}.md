### Author

Russell, Thomas Hawkes (1851-1913), British (English) solicitor and botanist. (*T.H. Russell*).

#### Herbarium and types

Russell had a private bryological herbarium.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 194; BB p 265; BM 4: 1770; CSP 18: 367; Desmond p. 535 (b. 30 Mar 1851, d. 31 Jul 1913); Kew 4: 553.

#### Biofile

- Jackson, B.D., Proc. Linn. Soc. 1913-14: 61 (obit.).
