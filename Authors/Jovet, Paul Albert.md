### Author

Jovet, Paul Albert (1896-x), French botanist, specialized in the flora of W. and S.W. France. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Jovet*

#### Herbarium and types

Private; material also at [[Collection P|P]], [[Collection PC|PC]], [[Collection RAB|RAB]].
