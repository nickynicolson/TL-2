### Author

Leimer, Franz (*fl*. 1854), German benedictine monk and botanist at Augsburg. (*Leim.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1084; PR 5189; Rehder 5: 500.
