### Author

Dekhuyzen, Marinus Cornelis (1859-1924), Dutch veterinarian, Dr. sci. Leiden 1884, professor at Utrecht (veterinary sciences), died 9 Oct 1924. (*Dekhuyzen*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
