### Author

Dietz, Sándor (also known as A. Dietz) (fl. 1887), Hungarian botanist, assistant at the chair of botany of the university. 
**Abbreviated name**: *S. Dietz*

[[Collection BP|BP]], some material at [[Collection B|B]].
