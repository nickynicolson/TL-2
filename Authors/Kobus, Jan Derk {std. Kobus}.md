### Author

Kobus, Jan Derk (1858-1910), Dutch botanist, at the Eastern Java Sugar Experiment Station, Pasuruan, Java. (*Kobus*).

#### Herbarium and types

Indonesian material at [[Collection BO|BO]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection PAS|PAS]]; Dutch material at [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection NBV|NBV]].
