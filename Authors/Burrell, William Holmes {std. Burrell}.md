### Author

Burrell, William Holmes (1865-1945), Britisch (London born) pharmacist and biologist, lived in Yorkshire 1914-1945, curator of the Ingham Herbarium, Leeds University. (*Burrell*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LDS|LDS]]. – Some further material at [[Collection OXF|OXF]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 288; BL 2: 258, 259; Clokie p. 142; Desmond p. 109 (main source, d. 30 Mar 1945); LS 4478, 31.778-31.778a.
