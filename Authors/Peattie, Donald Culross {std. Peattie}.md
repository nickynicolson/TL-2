### Author

Peattie, Donald Culross (1898-1964), American botanist; studied at Univ. Chicago (1915-1917; no degree). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Peattie*

#### Herbarium and types

material at [[Collection CHARL|CHARL]], [[Collection F|F]], [[Collection GH|GH]], [[Collection NCU|NCU]], [[Collection NY|NY]]. – Manuscript material in Smithsonian Archives.
