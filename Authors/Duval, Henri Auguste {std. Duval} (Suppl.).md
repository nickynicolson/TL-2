### Author

\* Duval, Henri Auguste (1777-1814), French physician and botanist, born 28 Apr 1777 at Alençon (Orne), died 16 Mar 1814 at Paris. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Duval*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
