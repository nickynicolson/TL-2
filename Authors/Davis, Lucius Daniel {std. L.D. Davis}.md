### Author

Davis, Lucius Daniel (1826-1900), Methodist Episcopal clergyman, born in Jerusalem, N.Y., later at New York, died 31 Oct 1900, Newport, RI. (*L.D. Davis*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
