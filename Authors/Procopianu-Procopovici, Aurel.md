### Author

Procopianu-Procopovici, Aurel (*fl*. 1890-1900), Roumanian botanist; inspector at the botanical garden, Bucarest (1896). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Procopianu*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection CERN|CERN]].

#### Bibliography and biography
