### Author

Schinnerl, Martin (1861-1950), German (Bavarian) bryologist and high school teacher at München; librarian Bayer. bot. Ges. 1890-1903; first secretary id. 1903-1905. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Schinnerl*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection M|M]].
