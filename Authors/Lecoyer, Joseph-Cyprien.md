### Author

Lecoyer, Joseph-Cyprien (1835-1899), Belgian botanist; high school teacher at Neufchâteau, Wavre and Ath; retired at Beauwelz. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lecoy.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]].
