### Author

Slater, Matthew B. (c. 1829-1918), British bryologist at Malton; friend of Richard Spruce. (*Slater*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection KGY|KGY]]. – *Letters* to F.F.G. Renauld at [[Collection PC|PC]]; other letters at [[Collection G|G]].
