### Author

Sakurai, Kyuichi (1889-1963), Japanese bryologist. (*Sakurai*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MAK|MAK]] (300.000); further material [[Collection B|B]] (ferns, extant) and [[Collection H|H]] (Bryo.). – 32 letters to V. Brotherus in [[Collection H|H]]-UB.
