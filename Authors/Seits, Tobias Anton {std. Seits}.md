### Author

Seits, Tobias Anton (1772-1833), Austrian botanist and clergyman; priest at Mondsee in Upper Austria. (*Seits*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 257 (b. 12 Sep 1772, d. 17 Mar 1833); Herder p. 342; Maiwald p. 269; PR 8595-8596, ed. 1: 9546-9547; Rehder 1: 119.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Flora 2(1): 366. 1819 (will issue his Europ. carpol.-fl. in Hefte).
