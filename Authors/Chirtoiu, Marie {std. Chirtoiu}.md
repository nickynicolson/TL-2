### Author

Chirtoiu, Marie (1892-x), Swiss botanist, studied with R. Chodat at Genève, Dr. ès. Sci. nat. 1917, married Henry Guyot. (*Chirtoiu*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 342; Kew 1: 538.
