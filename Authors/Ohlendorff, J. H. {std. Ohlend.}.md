### Author

Ohlendorff, J. H. (x-1857), German gardener at Hamburg. (*Ohlend.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 24; Hortus 3: 1201 ("Ohlend."); Kew 4: 179; Rehder 1: 47, 5: 632; Tucker 1: 528.
- Anon., Bot. Zeit. 15: 608. 28 Aug 1857; Flora 4(1): 125. 1821.
- Voigt, A., Bot. Inst. Hamburg 25, 34. 1897.

#### Eponymy

*Ohlendorffia* J. G. C. Lehmann ex Schlechtendal (1837) is very likely named for him but the author of the generic name gives no derivation.
