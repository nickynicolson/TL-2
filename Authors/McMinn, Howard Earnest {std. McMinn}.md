### Author

McMinn, Howard Earnest (1891-1963), American botanist, dendrologist and horticulturist; professor of botany in Mills College, Oakland, Calif. 
**Abbreviated name**: *McMinn*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection UC|UC]]; for further coll. see Kasapligil (1965).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 430 (b. 19 Aug 1891, d. 25 Aug 1963); BL 1: 162, 166, 307; Bossert p. 261; Hortus 3: 1199 ("McMinn"); IH 1 (ed. 6): 361, 2: 486; Kew 3: 547; Langman p. 492-493; NAF 286(2): 344; PH 209; Roon p. 76.
- Ewan, J., Leafl. W. Bot. 7: 76. 1953.
- Kasapligil, B., Madroño 18: 128. 1965 (herb.); Fremontia 1 (4): 9-12. 1974 (portr., tribute).
- Thomas, J. H., Madroño 17: 115. 1963 (d.).

#### Composite works

with Maunsell Van Rensselaer: *Ceanothus*, Santa Barbara 1942 (see e.g. C. H. Connors, J. New York Bot. Gard. 44: 23-24. 1943).
