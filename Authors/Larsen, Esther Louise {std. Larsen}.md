### Author

Larsen, Esther Louise (1901-x), American botanist trained at Washington University, St. Louis, the Missouri Botanical Garden and later at Univ. of Montana and Univ. of Pennsylvania; married (1934) Kenneth David Doak. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Larsen*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MO|MO]], [[Collection MONTU|MONTU]], [[Collection PENN|PENN]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 347; BJI 2: 101; Bossert p. 228; IH 2: 413; Kew 3: 369; Langman p. 430.
- Ewan, J. et al., Short hist. bol. U.S. 35. 1969.

#### Eponymy

*Larsenia* Bremekamp (1965) is dedicated to Kai Larsen (1926-x), Danish botanist. *Larseniella* Munk (1942) is dedicated to Paul Larsen (1864-1938), Danish secondary school teacher and mycologist.
