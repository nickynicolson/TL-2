### Author

Bley, Franz (1881-?), German agriculturist and botanist, born in Banaran, Semarang, Java, at the dutch school at Surakarta, Java, to Germany 1889, "Volksschule" Jever, Oldenburg, Gymnasium, "Volontär" nr. Jever on an agricultural estate, from Oct 1900 at the agricultural university and at the regular university of Halle, returned to Java 1905, to Zürich University 1908, from 1914-1920 again in Java, studied again in Zürich 1920, ib. Dr. phil. 1925. (*F. Bley*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
