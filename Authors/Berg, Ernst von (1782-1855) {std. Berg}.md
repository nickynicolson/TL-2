### Author

Berg, Ernst von (1782-1855), German landowner in Neuenkirchen in Mecklenburg-Strelitz. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Berg*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 1: 138; CSP 1: 288; Hortus 3: 1188; Kew 1: 220; PR 639-640, ed. 1: 731-732.
