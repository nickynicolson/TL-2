### Author

Tellam, Richard Vercoe (1826-1908), British farmer and naturalist in Cornwall. (*Tellam*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TRU|TRU]] (part of herb. cited in Fl. Cornwall (H. Davey, 1909)), algae at Bodmin Museum (fide L.M. Newton; some material (e.g. lichens) was lost during a flood in 1953: "a good deal of material, notably the marine algae" is still extant (R. Penhallurick in lit. to D.L. Hawksworth, 7 Dec 1976).
