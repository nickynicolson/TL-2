### Author

Smith, \[Mrs\] Anna Maria (*fl*. 1878), Austrian(?) botanist, active in and around Fiume. (*A.M. Sm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection FI|FI]]; further fungi in [[Collection BR|BR]] and [[Collection K|K]].

#### Bibliography and biography

CSP 11: 431; Jackson p. 266; Saccardo 1: 155, 2: 101.

#### Biofile

- Jackson, B.D., Bull. misc. Inf. Kew 1901: 61 (146 fungi from Fiume and Tirol).
- Verdoorn, F., ed., Chron. bot. 2: 332, 405. 1936, 3: 295. 1937, 4: 278. 1938, 5: 175, 307, 518. 1939.
