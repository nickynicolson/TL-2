### Author

Schulz, Franz (*fl*. 1869), German gardener and botanist at Berlin. (*F. Schultz.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Jackson p. 297, 299; Rehder 1: 294, 371, 386, 3: 69, 4: 83.
