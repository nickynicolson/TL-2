### Author

Drummond, James Lawson (1783-1853), British physician and naturalist in Belfast. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. L. Drumm.*

#### Herbarium and types

*Ref*.: Hedge and Lamond, Index coll. Edinburgh 76. 1970.
