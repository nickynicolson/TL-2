### Author

M'Mahon \[McMahon\], Bernard (c. 1775-1816), Irish-born American horticulturist and seedsman at Philadelphia; author of the first American gardener's guide. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M'Mahon*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PH|PH]].
