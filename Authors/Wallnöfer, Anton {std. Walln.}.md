### Author

Wallnöfer, Anton (*fl*. 1888), Austrian bryologist in Klagenfurt. (*Walln.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Had a large bryophyte herbarium (40.000 specimens), which was offered for sale by him in 1905 (Rev. bryol. 32(1): 16. 1905. – Some material by him is at [[Collection BM|BM]] (until 1969 at [[Collection K|K]]) and [[Collection QK|QK]].

#### Bibliography and biography

CSP 19: 457.
