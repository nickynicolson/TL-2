### Author

Giesenhagen, Karl Friedrich Georg (1860-1928), German botanist, curator of the cryptogamic collections at München (M). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Giesenh.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection M|M]]; – some musci at [[Collection H|H]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 224.
