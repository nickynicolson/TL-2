### Author

Dunk, Kurt von der (1897-1985), German botanist (bryologist) from Elberfeld, Dr. ph. Frankfurt am Main 1921. (*Dunk*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PRIV|PRIV]] (fide G. Sayre).
