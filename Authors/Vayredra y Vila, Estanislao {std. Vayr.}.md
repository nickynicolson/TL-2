### Author

Vayredra y Vila, Estanislao (1848-1901), Spanish (Catalonian) botanist and pharmacist at Olot, Gerona, Barcelona and Sagaro. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Vayr.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BC|BC]] (15.000). – *Rosa* in [[Collection BR|BR]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 3: 797; Barnhart 3: 428 (b. 11 Nov 1848, d. 20 Sep 1901); BL 2: 495, 501, 727; BM 5: 2197; Colmeiro 1: cciv; CSP 11: 682, 19: 307; Hortus 3: 1206; Morren ed. 10, p. 52; Rehder 5: 876; Tucker 1: 716.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Encicl. univ. ilustr. europ.-amer. 67: 360. 1929 (bibl., portr., biogr. sketch).
