### Author

Wilhelm, Rudolph (1857-?), German (West-Prussian) architect and botanist; studied at Berlin, München and Königsberg; Dr. phil. Königsberg 1885. 
**Abbreviated name**: *R. With.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
