### Author

Dix, Emily (fl. 1934), Britsh botanist who worked on the flora of the Upper Carboniferous. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Dix*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 458 (only name).
