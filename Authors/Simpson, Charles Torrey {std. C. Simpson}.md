### Author

Simpson, Charles Torrey (1826-1932), American malacologist and botanist; conchologist with the United States National Museum 1889-1902; collaborator USDA; retired to Florida, writing on Florida wild life. (*C. Simpson*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection A|A]] and [[Collection NY|NY]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 281 (b. 3 Jun 1846, d. 17 Dec 1932); Tucker 1: 652.

#### Eponymy

*Simpsonipollis* S.K. Srivastava (1975) was named for J.B. Simpson (1894-1960), Scottish geologist-palynologist.
