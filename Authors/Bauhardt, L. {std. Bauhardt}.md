### Author

Bauhardt, L. (fl. 1823), German pharmacist and botanist. (*Bauhardt*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 140; BM 1: 112; PR 499, ed. 1: 577.
