### Author

Koelpin, Alexander Bernhard (1739-1801), German physician, botanist and teacher at the academic gymnasium at Stettin. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Koelp.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]].

#### Bibliography and biography

#### Eponymy

*Koelpinia* Pallas (1776).
