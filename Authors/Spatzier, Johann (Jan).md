### Author

Spatzier, Johann \[Jan\] (1806-1883), Austrian-Silesian (Moravian) naturalist and pharmacist at Jägerndorf (Krnov) 1835-1883. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Spatzier*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BRNM|BRNM]], [[Collection OP|OP]] and see Klášterský p. 175.

#### Bibliography and biography

CSP 5: 768, 8: 987, 11: 459; Futak-Domin p. 556; Klášsterský p. 175 (b. 16 Mai 1806, d. 30 Jan 1883).
