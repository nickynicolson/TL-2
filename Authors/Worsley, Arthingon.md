### Author

Worsley, Arthingon (1861-1944), British civil engineer and amateur horticulturist; lived in Isleworth, Middlesex from 1894; specialist on Amaryllis. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Worsl.*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material in [[Collection K|K]].
