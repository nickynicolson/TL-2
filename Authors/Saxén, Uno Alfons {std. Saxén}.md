### Author

Saxén, Uno Alfons (1863-1948), Finnish high school teacher and amateur botanist; Dr. phil. Helsinki 1892 (*Saxén*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection H|H]] (mainly Hieracium and Taraxacum).

#### Biofile

- Hiitonen, I., Luonnon Tutkija 52: 51. 1948 (obit., portr.).
