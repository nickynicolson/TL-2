### Author

Ito, Keisuke (1803-1901), Japanese herbalist. (*K. Ito*).

#### Herbarium and types

A small collection at [[Collection L|L]]; no herbarium specimens seem to be extant in Japan (inf. H. Hara).

#### Eponymy
