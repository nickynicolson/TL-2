### Author

Eloffe, Arthur (1826-x), French naturalist (préparateur) and taxidermist, member of the imperial society and several horticultural societies. (*Eloffe*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 2: 524; Jackson p. 218, 275; LS 8005; Rehder 1: 35, 3: 175.
