### Author

Kern, Johannes Hendrikus (1903-1974), Dutch botanist, high school teacher at Nijmegen; later at the Rijksherbarium, Leiden, specialist on Cyperaceae. (*J. Kern*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection L|L]] (Dutch coll.), [[Collection BO|BO]] (Java).
