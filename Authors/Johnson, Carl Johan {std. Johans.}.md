### Author

Johnson, Carl Johan (1858-1888), Swedish mycologist; studied at Uppsala (1883 fil. kand., 1886 fil lie). (*Johans.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection C|C]], [[Collection H|H]], [[Collection LD|LD]], [[Collection UPS|UPS]].
