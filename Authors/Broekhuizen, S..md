### Author

Broekhuizen, S. (fl. 1925-1939), Dutch mycologist and phytopathologist, research assistant with the "Nederlandse Gist- en Spiritusfabriek" at Delft. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Broekhuizen*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
