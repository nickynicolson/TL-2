### Author

Embury, Emma Catherine \[n√©e Manley\] (1806-1863), American botanist, born 1806 New York City, married Daniel Embury in 1828, leader of a literary salon, as author contributed to various periodicals, died 10 Feb 1863. (*Embury*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
