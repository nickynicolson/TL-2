### Author

Langenbach, Gustav (1831-1873), German botanist; Dr. phil. Breslau 1863; later at Naples where he died of cholera. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Langenb.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. Langenbach states on p. viii of his *Meeresalgen* (see below) that C. I. L. Kny and he himself planned to deposit an algal herbarium, with sets of their collections, at [[Collection PAL|PAL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 342; BM 3: 1055; CSP 8: 160; Jackson p. 322; Saccardo 1: 94; Anon., Bot. Zeit. 31: 797. 1873.
- Cesati, V. de, Saggio bibl. algol. ital. 212. 1882.
