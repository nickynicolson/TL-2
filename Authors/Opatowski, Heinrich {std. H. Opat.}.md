### Author

Opatowski, Heinrich (1812-?), German physician and botanist in Saalfeld; Dr. med. Berlin 1838. (*H. Opat.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1472; Herder p. 339; Kew 4: 191; PR 6837, corr. (ed. 1: 7637); Rehder 5: 635.
