### Author

Ricca, Luigi (x-1879), Italian customs officer and botanist on the French-Italian border in western Liguria. (*Ricca*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection GE|GE]] (fide Cavillier 1941); other material at [[Collection FI|FI]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 150 (d. 15 Jul 1979 for 1879); BL 2: 363, 708; Kew 4: 458; Moebius p. 455; Roon p. 5: 723; Saccardo 1: 138, 2: 91.

#### Biofile

- Burnat, É., Bull. Soc.-bot. France 30: cxxix. 1893 (d. 1881).
- Cavillier, F., Boissiera 5: 79. 1941 (d. 15 Jul 1879).
