### Author

Audouit, Edmond (fl. 1847), French naval physician. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Audouit*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 89; BM 1: 71; NI 54n; PR 282-283.
