### Author

Bruch, Carl \[Karl\] Ludwig (fl. 1758), German physician from Bergzabern, Pfalz, studied at the University of Strasbourg, Dr. med. ib. 22 Mai 1758. (*C.L. Bruch*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
