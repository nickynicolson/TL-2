### Author

Richter, Berthold (1834-?), German (Silesian) botanist; Dr. med. Breslau 1860. 
**Abbreviated name**: *B. Richt.*

#### Herbarium and types

In 1915 in the Upper Silesian Museum in Gleiwitz (Werner, 1915).
