### Author

Jones, Herbert Lyon (1866-1898), American botanist; Harvard AB 1892, instructor in botany Harvard 1895-1897; professor of botany at Oberlin College 1897-1898. 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. L. Jones*

[[Collection CU|CU]].
