### Author

Schulz, Paul Franz Ferdinand (1872-1919), German (Berlin) botanist; teacher at various Berlin schools; popular writer on cultivated plants. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P.F.F. Schulz*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Schulz ran a "Tauschvermittlung für Herbarpflanzen" and published annual catalogues (at least 1899-1904) in which herbarium material was offered for sale or exchange. The "4. jährl. Verzeichniss" issued 1 Jan 1903, for instance, offered not less than 7500 different taxa coming from 90 countries. Many cultivated plants were included. – 36 Fungi were at [[Collection B|B]] (rd 1903).
