### Author

Cels, Jean François (1810-1888), French horticulturist, son of François Cels, continued his father’s establishment. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.F. Cels*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Bret. p. 248; Hortus 3: 1190; Kew 1: 501.
