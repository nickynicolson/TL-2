### Author

Marshall, Joseph Jewison (1860-1934), British bryologist and pharmaceutical chemist 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Marsh.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection HUL|HUL]] (destroyed); bryophytes with Lincolnshire Naturalist's Union (fide Desmond).
