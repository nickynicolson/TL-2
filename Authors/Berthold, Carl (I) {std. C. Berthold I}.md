### Author

Berthold, Carl \[I\] (1834?-1884), German botanist and highschool teacher at Brilon (Arnsberg, Preussen). (*C. Berthold I*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 1: 153; PR 716-717.
