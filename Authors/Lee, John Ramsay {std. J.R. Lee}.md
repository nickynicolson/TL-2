### Author

Lee, John Ramsay (1868-1959), Scottish cashier and amateur botanist at Glasgow. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. R. Lee*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection GL|GL]].

### Publications

##### n.4291. Flora of the Clyde area

**Title**
*Flora of the Clyde area* a handbook of the flowering plants and ferns occuring wild or established within the drainage area of the river and firth of Clyde by John R. Lee. Glasgow (John Smith & Son (Glasgow) Ltd.) 1933. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Fl. Clyde*.

**Notes**
*Publ*.: before Jul 1933 (J. Bot. 1933; Nat. Nov. Sep 1933), p. \[i\]-xvi, \[1\]-391. *Copy*: MO. – Replaces Hennedy, *Clydesdale Flora* (eds. 1-5, 1865-1891) (TL-2/2640). – See also Rehder 5: xxi for his *Plants of the Kenmuir district of the Clyde* (1898).
*Ref*.: Matthews, J. Bot. 71: 202-204. 1933.
