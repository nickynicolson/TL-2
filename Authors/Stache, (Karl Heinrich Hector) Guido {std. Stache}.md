### Author

Stache, \[Karl Heinrich Hector\] Guido (1833-1921), German (Silesian) geologist and palaeontologist; Dr. phil. Breslau 1855; from 1857-1902 with the Geologische Reichsanstalt, Wien, from 1892 as director. (*Stache*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
