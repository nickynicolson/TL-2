### Author

Krauer, Johann Georg (1794-1845), Swiss botanist and physician at Luzern and Rothenburg. (*Krauer*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 576, 577, 676; BM 2: 1024; IH 2: 386; NI 1099; PR 4859 (ed. 1: 5391); Rehder 5: 472.
