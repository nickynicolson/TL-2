### Author

Storm, Vilhelm \[Ferdinand Johan\] (1835-1913), Norwegian botanist and zoologist; studied at the University of Oslo; curator of the Trondhjem Museum 1856-1913. (*Storm*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TRH|TRH]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 336 (b. 28 Sep 1835, d. 19 Mai 1913); BL 2: 456, 720; BM 5: 2030; IH 2: (files); Kleppa p. 10, 261, 282; Rehder 1: 356, 357.
