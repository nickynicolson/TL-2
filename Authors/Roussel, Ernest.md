### Author

Roussel, Ernest (*fl*. 1860), French pharmacist and mycologist at Évreux; studied at the École de médecine et de pharmacie de Rouen (thesis 1860). 
**Abbreviated name**: *E. Roussel*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
