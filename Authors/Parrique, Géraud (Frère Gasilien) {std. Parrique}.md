### Author

Parrique, Géraud ("Frère Gasilien") (1851-1907), French school teacher, clergyman, lichenologist and bryologist at Sorbiers (Loire) 
**Abbreviated name**: *Parrique*

#### Herbarium and types

Material at [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection H|H]], [[Collection PC|PC]] and [[Collection W|W]]. – The original herbarium was owned by Bouly de Lesdain at Dunkerque \[Duinkerken\], France, but destroyed in 1940. After Frère Gasilien changed his name back to Parrique, he formed a new herbarium (Gyelnik 1939). Its location is not known to us.
