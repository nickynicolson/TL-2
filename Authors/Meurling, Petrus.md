### Author

Meurling, Petrus (*fl*. 1820), Swedish physician. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Meurling*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1295.

### Publications

##### n.5893. De Vegetatione Scaniae dissertatio

**Title**
*De Vegetatione Scaniae dissertatio*. Quam venia ampl. fac. phil. Lund. publice examinandam proponunt Mag. Joh. Forsander & Petrus Meurling. Smolandi. In Academia Carolina d. xix Dec mdcccxx. Lundae \[Lund\] (ex Officina Berlingiana) 1820 Qu.
**Abbreviated title**: *Veg. Scan.*

**Notes**
*Co-author*: Johann Forsander (1795-1866), Swedish high school teacher; senior author. The publication is attributed to Forsander by Krok; Meurling was probably merely a codefendent. See KR p. 191-192.
*Publ*.: 19 Dec 1820, p. \[1\]-16. *Copies*: G(2).
