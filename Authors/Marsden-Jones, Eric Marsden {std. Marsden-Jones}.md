### Author

Marsden-Jones, Eric Marsden (1887-1960), British experimental taxonomist; established the Potterne Biological Station, near Devizes, Wiltshire 1923 (-1940); collaborated with W. B. Turrill at Kew. (*Marsden-Jones*).
