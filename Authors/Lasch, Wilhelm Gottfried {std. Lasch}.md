### Author

Lasch, Wilhelm Gottfried (1787-1863), German pharmacist, mycologist, and hieraciologist at Driesen (Driessen) (Neumark); from 1814-1824 practicing pharmacist; from 1824 Privatgelehrter. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lasch*
