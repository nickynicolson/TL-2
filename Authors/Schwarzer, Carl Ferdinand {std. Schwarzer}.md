### Author

Schwarzer, Carl Ferdinand (1829-1870), German (Silesian) veterinarian and botanist; studied veterinary science at the Royal Veterinary College at Berlin; active service in the Prussian army 1851-1852; practicing veterinarian at LÃ¶wen (Upper Silesia) 1852-1854, from 1854 idem at Kuhnern near Striegau (Silesia); active batologist.
