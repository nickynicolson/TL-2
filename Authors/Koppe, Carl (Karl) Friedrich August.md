### Author

Koppe, Carl \[Karl\] Friedrich August (1803-1874), German botanist and high school teacher at Soest. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Koppe*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 2: 1016; Dryander 4: 222; Kew 3: 298.
