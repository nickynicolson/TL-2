### Author

Sulzberger, Robert (*fl*. 1888), Belgian botanist and school teacher. (*Sulzberger*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2048; Rehder 2: 282; Tucker 1: 673.
