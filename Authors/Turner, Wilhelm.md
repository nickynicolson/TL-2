### Author

Turner, Wilhelm (1860-?), German (Bremen) botanist; studied in Göttingen and Freiburg i. Br. 1882-1885; Dr. phil. Göttingen 1885. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Turn.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Langman p. 749; Rehder 2: 565.
