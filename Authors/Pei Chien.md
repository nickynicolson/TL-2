### Author

P'ei Chien (1903-x), Chinese botanist; AB Stanford 1927; AM ib. 1928, studied in U.S. 1925-1930, later at the Biological Laboratory of the Science Society of China, Nanking. 
**Abbreviated name**: *P'ei*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NSM|NSM]].
