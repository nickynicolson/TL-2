### Author

Szafran, Bronislaw (1897-1968), Polish botanist (bryologist) at the Agricultural Station, Lwów. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Szafran*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection KRA|KRA]].

#### Note

Published *Flora polska Mchy* (Musci), 2 vols., 1957, 1961.
