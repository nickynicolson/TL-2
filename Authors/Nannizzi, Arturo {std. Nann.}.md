### Author

Nannizzi, Arturo (1877-1961), Italian botanist; lecturer in mycology at the University of Siena; from 1935 also director of the Siena botanical garden. (*Nann.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection SIENA|SIENA]].

#### Bibliography and biography

- Ciferri, R., Taxon 1(8): 127. 1952.
