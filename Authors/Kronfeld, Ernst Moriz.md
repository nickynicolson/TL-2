### Author

Kronfeld, Ernst Moriz (1865-1942), Austro-Hungarian botanist; born at Lemberg (Lwow); editor of the Vienna *Fremdenblatt*. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Kronf.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
