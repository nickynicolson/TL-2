### Author

Dannenberg, Ingeborg (later known as Markgraf-Dannenberg) (fl. 1938), Swiss botanist who worked with Fr. Markgraf at the Botanical Museum in Berlin-Dahlem. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Markgr.-Dann.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
