### Author

Well, Johann Jacob von (1725-1787), Bohemian-born Austrian mineralogist and botanist; Dr. med. Wien; professor of natural history at Univ. Wien from 1760. (*Well*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2289; Bossert p. 430; Dryander 3: 24; PR 10157, ed. 1: 11141; Rehder 1: 83; WU 54: 225-226 (b. 1 Mar 1725, d. 4 Apr 1787).
