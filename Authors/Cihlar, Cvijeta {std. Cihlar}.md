### Author

Cihlar, Cvijeta (fl. 1915), Croatian botanist at Zagreb. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Cihlar*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 348 (cites Acta Bot. Zagr. 1: 150); LS suppl. 5184.
