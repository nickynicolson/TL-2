### Author

List, Friedrich Ludwig (fl. 1837), German botanist and high school teacher at Tilsit, Western Prussia. 
**Abbreviated name**: *List*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection KBG|KBG]] (destroyed).

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1154; PR 5542-5543 (ed. 1: 6189-6190); Rehder 5: 517.
- Candolle, Alph. de, Phytographie 429. 1880.

#### Eponymy

*Listia* E. H. F. Meyer (1836).

**Title**
I. *Plantae lithuanae*, quae chloridi borussici cl: Hagenii inserendae sunt. II. Salicum, quae prope Tilsam sponte crescunt, adumbrationes... Jahresbericht über das Königliche Gymnasium zu Tilsit für das Schuljahr Michaeli 1835/36... Tilsit (Gedruckt bei Johann Heinrich Post) 1837. Qu.
