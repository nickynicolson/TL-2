### Author

Philip, Robert Harris (1852-1912), British diatomist at Hull. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Philip*

#### Herbarium and types

Philip's own collections, as well as those by G. Norman catalogued by Philip, were in the Hull museum, but burnt in World War II. (Newton 1952).
