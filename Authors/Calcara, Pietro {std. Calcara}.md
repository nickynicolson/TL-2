### Author

Calcara, Pietro (1819-1854), Italian biologist, studied at the University of Palermo, scientist, later professor of botany and zoology at this same university. (*Calcara*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection FI|FI]].

#### Bibliography and biography
