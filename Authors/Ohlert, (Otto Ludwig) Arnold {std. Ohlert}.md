### Author

Ohlert, \[Otto Ludwig\] Arnold (1816-1875), German lichenologist and "Regierungsund Schulrath" at Danzig. (*Ohlert*).

#### Herbarium and types

Originally at Königsberg (now Kaliningrad), destroyed; some material still at [[Collection MW|MW]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 24; CSP 4: 665, 8: 527; GR cat. p. 69; Hawksworth p. 185; Herder p. 259; Jackson p. 160; Kelly p. 163; Kew 4: 179; LS 19442-19447; PR 6818, 16612; SBC p. 128.
- Candolle, Alph. de, Phytographie 437. 1880 (types KBG).
- Laundon, J. R., Lichenologist 11: 15. 1979 \[cites Kaliningrad for coll. lichens but our information is that all old Königsberg material was destroyed\].
- Szymkiewicz, D., Bibl. fl. Polsk. 47. 1925.
