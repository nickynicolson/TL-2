### Author

Nägeli, Otto (1871-1938), Swiss botanist and physician; habil. int. med. Zurich 1900; professor of medicine at Tübingen 1912-1918; id. at Zürich 1918 (from 1921 as ord. prof.). 
**Abbreviated name**: *O. Näg.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 535; BFM 1006, 1007, 1271; BJI 2: 122; BL 2: 582, 583, 591, 694; BM 3: 1389; Hirsch p. 209; Kew 4: 102; Rehder 1: 438.
- Rübel, E., Gesch. naturf. Ges. Zürich 101-102. 1947 (portr.; was haematologist: "Blut-Naegeli").
- Schmid, H. & C. Kummer, Mitt. naturf. Ges. Schaffhausen 24: 185-210. 1938 (obit.).

### Publications

##### n.6619. Die Flora des Kantous Zürich

**Title**
*Die Flora des Kantous Zürich*... Zurich (1: Verlag von Albert Raustein, 2: no imprint) 1905-1912, 2 parts. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Fl. Zürich*.

**Notes**
*Co-author* vol. 1: Albert Thellung (1881-1928).
*1*: Nov-Dec 1905 (ÖbZ. Nov-Dec 1905; Nat. Nov. Mar(1) 1906; Bot. Zeit. 1 Apr 1906), p. \[i-ii\], \[1\]-82.
*2*: 27 Jan 1912 (t.p.), p. \[1\]-61.
*Copy*: G. – Vol. 1 reprinted from Vierteljahrsschr. naturf. Ges. Zürich 50: 225-305; vol. 2 from Ber. Zürcher bot. Ges. 11: 1-61. 1912. Contains bibliography and Pteridophyta by Martin Albert Rikli (1868-1951); Nägeli acted simply as editor. Vol. 1 deals with the "Ruderal- und Adventivflora."
*Ref*.: Diels, L., Bot. Jahrb. 38(Lit.): 51-52. 1907.
