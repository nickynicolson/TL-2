### Author

Maillard, Pierre Néhémie (1813-1883), French botanist; clergyman at la Mothe-St.-Héray (Deux-Sèvres) 1842-1883. (*Maillard*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection CGE|CGE]] (via Genevier herb.); other mat. [[Collection DBN|DBN]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2:437 (b. 27 Jul 1813, d. 22 Apr 1883); BL 2: 145, 687; BM 3: 1223; Jackson p. 283; Kew 3: 565; Plesch p. 208; Render 5: 539; Tucker 1: 455.
- Andrieu, J., Bibl. gen. de L'Agenais 2: 102. 1887, 3: 125. 1891 (bibl.).
- Anon., Bull. Soc. bot. Deux-Sèvres 17 *pl. 3.* 1906 (portr.).
- Nelson, E.C., Glasra 4:54. 1980 (coll. DBN).
- Souché, B., Flore du Haut Poitou xiv. 1901.

#### Composite works

With Sauzé, J.C.: *Catalogue des plantes phanérogames... des Deux-Sèvres*, 1864, and *Flore du département des Deux-Sèvres* 1878-1884, see under Sauzé.

#### Eponymy

*Maillardia* Frappei & Duchartre (1862) is dedicated to L. Maillard, author of *Notes sur l'Île de la Réunion*, about whom we have no further information (Barnhart 2: 437; BM 3: 1223; PR 5745).
