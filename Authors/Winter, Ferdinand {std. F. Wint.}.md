### Author

Winter, Ferdinand (1835-1888), German (Prussian) pharmacist at Saarbrücken, later at Gerolstein (Eifel). (*F. Wint.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Mosses in [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.); further material in [[Collection BHU|BHU]], [[Collection M|M]], [[Collection MW|MW]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]].
*Exsiccatae*, with F.W. Schultz, *Herbarium normale* ser. 2, see under F.W. Schultz for sets in public herbaria.

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 6(1): 510; Backer p. 628; Barnhart 3: 508 (d. 3 Aug 1888); Clokie p. 266; CSP 8: 1253, 11: 828, 12: 787; PR 10338; Rehder 5: 926; TL-2/11.296, 11.304, see F.W. Schultz; Tucker 1: 750; Urban-Berl. 262, 317, 411.
