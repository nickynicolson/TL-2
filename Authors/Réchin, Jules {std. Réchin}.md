### Author

Réchin, Jules (1853-1913), French clergyman and botanist, teacher at Précigni and, from 1876, at the "Collège de Mamers". (*Réchin*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection ANGUC|ANGUC]] (bryoph.).

#### Bibliography and biography
