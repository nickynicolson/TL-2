### Author

Tremols y Borrell, Federico (1831-1900), Spanish (Catalonian) botanist and pharmaceutical chemist; professor of inorganic chemistry at Granada, later prof. of the history of pharmacy at Barcelona. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tremols*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BC|BC]] (ca. 8000); further material in [[Collection AWH|AWH]], [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.), [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection F|F]], [[Collection GOET|GOET]], [[Collection MAF|MAF]], [[Collection MW|MW]], [[Collection W|W]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]], [[Collection UPS|UPS]]-UB.
