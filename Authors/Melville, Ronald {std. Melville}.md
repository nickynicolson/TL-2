### Author

Melville, Ronald (1903-x), British botanist; Cheshunt Exp. Sta. 1930-1933; at the Royal Botanic Gardens, Kew, from 1934; specialist on the Australasian flora and British *Rosa*. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Melville*
