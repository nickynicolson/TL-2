### Author

Klášterský, Ivan (1901-197.), Czechoslovak botanist, especially rhodologist, curator of PR 1925-1955, later working at the Geobotanical laboratory of the Ceskosl. Akad. ved., Pruhonice. (*Klášt.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PR|PR]].
