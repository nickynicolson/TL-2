### Author

Savigny, \[Marie\] Jules César \[Lelorgne de\] (1777-1851), French botanist and zoologist; participated in Napoléon's visit to Egypt 1798-1802. (*Savigny*).

#### Herbarium and types

some material at [[Collection H|H]].

#### Bibliography and biography
