### Author

Singer, Max (*fl*. 1885), Austrian rhodologist, botanist and high school teacher; from 1898 professor at the German Gymnasium in the "Königl. Weinbergen" in Praha. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M. Singer*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 282; Biol.-Dokum. 17: 8702; Herder p. 122; Rehder 3: 450; Tucker 1: 652.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Österr. bot. Z. 48: 367. 1898 (prof. Gymn. k. Weinbergen, Praha).
- Dörfler, I., Botaniker-Adressbuch 230. 1909.
