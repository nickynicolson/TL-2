### Author

Schulze, Carl (1865-?), German (Holstein) botanist and pharmacist; pharmaceutical state exam. 1891; Dr. phil. Erlangen 1895. (*C. Schulze*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 246 (b. 28 Aug 1865).
