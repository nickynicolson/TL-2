### Author

Crookall, Robert (1889/1890-?), British palaeobotanist and geologist, studied at the University of Bristol, Dr. phil. 1925, D.Sc. 1932. (*Crookall*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Andrews ed. 2: 250; Barnhart 1: 397; Kew 1: 648.
