### Author

Wanner, Stefan (*fl*. 1885), Swiss botanist and high school teacher at Zürich. (*Wanner*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 457; DTS 1: 319; Rehder 1: 432; Tucker 1: 730.
