### Author

Rivière, Charles Marie (1845-?), French horticulturist; brother of Auguste Rivière; director of the experimental garden of Hamma, Algeria (asst. dir. 1868-1877; dir. from 1877). 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Rivière*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
