### Author

Nowell, John (1802-1867), British handloom weaver and bryologist at Todmorden, Yorkshire. (*Nowell*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TOD|TOD]] (not traced); further material at [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]] and [[Collection OXF|OXF]].

#### Bibliography and biography

#### Composite works

The mosses of the county, *in* J. G. Baker and J. Nowell, *Suppl. fl. Yorkshire*, see TL-2/262 under H. Baines.

#### Eponymy

*Nowellia* Mitten (1870). *Note*: *Nowellia* F. L. Stevens (1924) was named in honor of W. Nowell, a researcher on fungi in Trinidad.
