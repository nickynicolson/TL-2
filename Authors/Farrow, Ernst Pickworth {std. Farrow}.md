### Author

Farrow, Ernst Pickworth (fl. 1912-1941), British botanist and agriculturalist, B.S. Agriculture 1912, D. Sci. 1916 Univ. of London. (*Farrow*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Some mss. material in Cambridge Univ. Library, Cambridge, England.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 528; GR p. 397; Kew 2: 237; Tucker 3: 103 (as "W. Pickworth Farrow").
