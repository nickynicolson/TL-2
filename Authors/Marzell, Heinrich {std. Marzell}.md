### Author

Marzell, Heinrich (1885-1970), German botanist, classicist and linguist; Dr. phil. München 1911; from 1918-1950 high school teacher at the Realschule Gunzenhausen; Leibnitz Medaille 1954. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Marzell*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

M'S main work is his *Wörterbuch der deutschen Pflanzennamen* of which the first instalment (*Abelia-Agrimonia*) appeared in 1937, the first volume (*Abelia-Cytisus*) was completed in 1943 and of which Licferung 35 (Band 4, Lief. 6, *Trigonella-Vaccinium*) was published in 1978 by S. Hirzel Verlag, Stuttgart (originally: Leipzig). The book is now edited, on the basis of M's manuscript left on his death, by Heinz Paul.
