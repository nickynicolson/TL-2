### Author

Vendrely, \[François\] Xavier (1837-1908), French botanist and pharmacist; studied at the École de Pharmacie, Strasbourg 1856-1857; military service 1858-1859; continued his pharmaceutical studies in Besançon 1860-1861; state exam. pharm. ib. 1861; practicing pharmacist in Champagney 1861-1901. (*Vendrely*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]] (rd. 1975; inf. G. Aymonin); further material (fungi) at [[Collection W|W]]. – Issued (with others) several sets of *Flora cryptogama sequaniae exsiccata* (see under Justin Paillot, TL-2/4: 8) and IH 2: 636). See Aymonin (1976) for details of Vendrely's collections (now at [[Collection P|P]]). – *Letters* to F.F.G. Renauld at [[Collection P|P]]; other letters at [[Collection G|G]].
