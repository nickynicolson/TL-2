### Author

Schmitt, Joseph (1862-1915), French physician and botanist; Dr. phil. Paris 1904, professor at the Faculté de Médecine, Nancy. (*J. Schmitt*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection QPH|QPH]] (Robitaille 1930).

#### Bibliography and biography
