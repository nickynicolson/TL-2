### Author

Crampton, Cecil Burleigh (1871-1920), British (Scottish) botanist and geologist, in H.M. Geological survey, collected algae in S. Africa. (*C.B. Crampton*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MANCH|MANCH]] (alg.).
