### Author

Àvila y Zumarán, Pedro de (1842-?), Spanish botanist, senior engineer ("Ingeniero Jefe") with the Cuerpo de Ingenieros de Montes, at El Escorial. (*Àvila*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 94 (further biogr. refs.); BL 2: 482; BM 1: 78; Rehder 4: 544; TL-2/4119, 4121; Tucker 1: 46.

#### Composite works

With Máximo Laguna y Villanueva (1826-1902), see TL-2/2: 725, *Fl. forest. españ.* 1875 and 1883-1890, see TL-2/4119 and 4121, Àvila junior author. – Detailed reviews of the 1883-1890 edition by F.G.M.T. von Herder in Bot. Centralbl. 23(28): 48-51. 6-10 Jul 1885 and by M. Willkomm, ib. 50(28/29): 83-84. 12 Jul 1892.
