### Author

Suchlandt, Otto (1873-1947), German (East-Prussian) pharmacist, limnologist and botanist; founded the Rhätische Apotheke in Davos 1911; Dr. phil. Lausanne 1917. (*Suchlandt*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
