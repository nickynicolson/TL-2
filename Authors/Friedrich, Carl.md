### Author

Friedrich, Carl (1857-1928?), Latvian botanist (cryptogamist), born 19 Mai 1857 in Riga, Dr. Sci. 1878 Tübingen, Germany (unter S. Schwendener). 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. Friedrich*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
