### Author

Knight, Charles (1818-1891/92), British administrator and botanist (lichenologist) in New Zealand, ultimately auditor general. (*C. Knight*).

#### Herbarium and types

Personal herbarium at [[Collection WELTU|WELTU]]. New Zealand lichens at [[Collection BM|BM]] (first set?), [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection H|H]], [[Collection KIEL|KIEL]], [[Collection LD|LD]], [[Collection NY|NY]], [[Collection SAM|SAM]]„ [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]]; other material also at [[Collection K|K]].
