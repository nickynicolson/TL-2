### Author

Meulenhoff, J. S. (1867-1936), Dutch mycologist; president of the Nederlandse mycologische Vereniging 1919-1933 
**Abbreviated name**: *Meulenhoff*

#### Herbarium and types

Some fungi (coll. 1912-1920) in [[Collection L|L]].
