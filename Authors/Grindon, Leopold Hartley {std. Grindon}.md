### Author

Grindon, Leopold Hartley (1818-1904), British naturalist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Grindon*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MANCH|MANCH]] (21.000).
*Ref*.: IH 2: 240.
- Kent, British herbaria 58. 1957.

Barnhart 2: 88; BB p. 132; PR 3581-3582.
