### Author

Trouette, Édouard (1855-?), French (Réunion-born) pharmacist; Pharm. I. Cl. Paris 1879. (*Trouette*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Rehder 3: 717; Tucker 1: 701.

#### Biofile

- Lacroix, A., Figures de savants 4: 40. 1938.

#### Eponymy

*Trouettia* Pierre ex Baillon (1891) may have been for this author, but no etymology was given.
