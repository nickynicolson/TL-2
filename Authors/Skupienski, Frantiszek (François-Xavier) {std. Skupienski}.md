### Author

Skupienski, Frantiszek \[François-Xavier\] (1888-x), Polish botanist; Dr. phil. Univ. Paris 1920; with the Department of general botany of the Warsaw Polytechnic. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Skupienski*

Unknown.
