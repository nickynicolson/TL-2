### Author

Gleason, Henry Allan (1882-1975). American botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Gleason*

#### Herbarium and types

mostly at [[Collection NY|NY]]. See IH for details.
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 359, 2: 227.
- Cronquist, Garden J. 26: 56-59. 1976 (portr.)
