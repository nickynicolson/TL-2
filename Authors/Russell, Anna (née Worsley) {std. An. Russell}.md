### Author

Russell, Anna (née Worsley) (1807-1876), British (English) mycologist at Kenilworth; married Frederick Russell. (*An. Russell*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection OXF|OXF]]. – 733 original water-colour drawings of higher fungi at [[Collection BM|BM]].
