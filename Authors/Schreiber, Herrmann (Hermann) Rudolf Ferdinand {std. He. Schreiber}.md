### Author

Schreiber, Herrmann \[Hermann?\] Rudolf Ferdinand (1811-1853), German (Prussian) botanist. (*He. Schreiber*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 240 (b. 5 Nov 1811, d. 15 Apr 1853); CSP 5: 547; Tucker 1: 636.

#### Note

*Flora der Umgegend von Graben und Ludwigslust*, in Arch. Ver. Fr. Naturgesch. Meklenburg 7: 200-254. 1853 (rev. Bot. Zeit. 11 Nov 1853).
