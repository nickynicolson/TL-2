### Author

Leclercq, Suzanne Céline Marie (1901-x), Belgian palaeobotanist, specialized in palaeozoic botany and palynology. (*Leclercq*).

#### Herbarium and types

Palaeobotanical collections of the Université de Liège.

#### Bibliography and biography

Roon p. 67.
- Banks, H. P., Rev. Palaeobot. Palyn. 14(1-2): v, 1-5. 1972 (portr., bibl.).

#### Eponymy
