### Author

Retzius, \[Magnus\] Gustaf (1842-1919), Swedish physician and zoologist; Dr. med. Stockholm 1871; from 1871 at the Karolinska inst.; professor of histology (later: anatomy) 1877-1890; son of A.A. Retzius. (*G. Retz.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Eponymy

The Hierta-Retzius Foundation was established by Retzius and his wife (b. Hierta) with the Swedish Academy of Sciences to further scientific research and social progress.
