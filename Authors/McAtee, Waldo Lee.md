### Author

McAtee, Waldo Lee (1883-x), American entomologist, botanist and ornithologist, A. M. Indiana Univ. 1906; with USDA in various functions 1904-1947. 
**Abbreviated name**: *McAtee*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection US|US]]; further material at [[Collection NY|NY]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 118 (b. 21 Jan 1883); BL 1: 132, 172, 173, 185, 188, 205, 306; GR p. 230-231; IH 2: 476; Pennell p. 613; Tucker 1: 447.
- Osborn, H., Fragm. entom. hist. 107, 120, 213, 340. 1937 (portr.).

#### Note
