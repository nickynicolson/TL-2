### Author

Becker, Hans (1884-?), German botanist, born in Halle, Westfalen, educated at the Haller Volksschule at Halle, id. at the "höheren Privatschule" ib. 1896-1900, at the Gymnasium at Bielefeld and Hagen in Westfalen 1900-1906, studied at the universities of Tübingen (semester 1), Berlin (id. 2), München (id. 3), Berlin (id. 4-5) and ultimately 1908-1911/12 in Münster i.W., Dr. phil. at the Westfälische Wilhelms-Universität 16 Dec 1911, later Forstassessor at the Kön. techn. Hochschule, München for "Bodenkunde". 
**Abbreviated name**: *H. Becker*
