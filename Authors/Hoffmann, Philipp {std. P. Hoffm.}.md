### Author

Hoffmann, Philipp (*fl*. 1868), German botanist; high school teacher at the Lyceum of Eichstätt. (*P. Hoffm.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 2: 858; Jackson p. 300, 303; Kew 3: 9; PR 4160; Rehder 5: 398; Tucker 1: 346.
