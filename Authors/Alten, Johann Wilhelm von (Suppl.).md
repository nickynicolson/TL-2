### Author

\* Alten, Johann Wilhelm von (1770-1854), German pharmacist, botanist and malacologist at Augsburg (Bayern), member of the Regensburgische Botanische Gesellschaft 1807. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.W. v. Alten*

#### Herbarium and types
