### Author

Tettelbach, \[Ernst Gustav\] Moritz (1794-1870), German (Saxonian) botanical artist and engraver at Dresden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tettelbach*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

NI 1354, 1947; see also 1: 195, 211; PR 10140, ed. 1: 6184; Rehder 1: 85.

#### Biofile

- Helm, J., Acta hist. Leop. 3: 13-14. *pl. 2* (portr.), 50-51. 1965 (Die Pillnitzer Centurienbande; biogr. details).

#### Composite works

- (1) Artist, G.F.W. Meyer, *Fl. Hannover* 1842-1854, TL-2/5935.
- (2) Artist *Plantae selectae vivis coloribus depictae* (see Helm 1965).
