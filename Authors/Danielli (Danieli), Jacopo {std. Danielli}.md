### Author

Danielli \[Danieli\], Jacopo (fl. 1885), Italian anthropologist, "libera docente" at Firenze. (*Danielli*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 417 ("Danieli"); Kew 2: 10; Saccardo p. 60 ("Danieli").
