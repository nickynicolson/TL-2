### Author

Amberg, Karl (1886-1940), Swiss plant geographer at Engelberg; Dr. phil. Zürich (E.T.H.) 1916. (*Amberg*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 48; BL 2: 577, 622 (dates 1886-1940).
