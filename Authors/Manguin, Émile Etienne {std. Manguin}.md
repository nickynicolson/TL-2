### Author

Manguin, Émile Etienne (1893-1966), French algologist at the Muséum d'Histoire naturelle, Paris (*Manguin*).

#### Herbarium and types

Types of algae at [[Collection PC|PC]]; bryophyte and lichen herbarium at [[Collection CLF|CLF]].
