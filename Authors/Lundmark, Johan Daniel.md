### Author

Lundmark, Johan Daniel (1755-1792). Swedish physician and botanist; studied at Uppsala 1774-1780; Dr. med. Uppsala 1788; regional physician of Filipstads district (1783) 1784. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lundmark*

#### Herbarium and types

Herbarium at the Karsbad Gymnasium; further material at [[Collection SBT|SBT]] and [[Collection UPS|UPS]] (Thunberg herb.).

#### Bibliography and biography

IH 2: 469; KR p. 474; PR 5694 ed. 1: 6368; Rehder 5: 528; SO 1317, 3829-3831; TL-1/772; Tucker 1: 415.

#### Thesis

(for med. doctor's degree): *Disserlatio de usu Linnaeae medico*. Praeside J. G. Acrel, Upsaliae 1788, Qu. 10 p.
