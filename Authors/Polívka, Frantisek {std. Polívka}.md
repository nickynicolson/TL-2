### Author

Polívka, Frantisek (1860-1923), Czech botanist; high school teacher at Olomouc (Olmütz). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Polívka*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; the large Polivka herbarium at [[Collection PR|PR]] is of Jaroslav Polivka (1893-x).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 96; Futák-Domin p. 483; Klášterský p. 147 (q.v. for further biogr. refs.).
