### Author

Dugas, Marguerite (fl. 1928), French botanist at Paris, Docteur ès sciences naturelles 1928 at the faculty of sciences of the University of Paris. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Dugas*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
