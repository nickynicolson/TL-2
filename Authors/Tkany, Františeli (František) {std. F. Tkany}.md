### Author

Tkany, Františeli \[František\] (1851-?), Moravian botanist and high school teacher at Olomouc (Olmütz). 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. Tkany*

#### Herbarium and types

Gymnasium Olomouc (fide Klášterský).

#### Bibliography and biography

Futak-Domin p. 610; Jackson p. 268, 506; Klášterský p. 186; Rehder 1: 442.
