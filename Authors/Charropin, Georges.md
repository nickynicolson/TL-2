### Author

Charropin, Georges (fl. 1873), French pharmacist and botanist, born at Pons, Charente-Inférieure, at the École supérieure de pharmacie de Paris. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Charropin*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
