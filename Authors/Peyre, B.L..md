### Author

Peyre, B.L. (*fl*. 1823), French botanist, army surgeon with the 18th "Régiment d'Infanterie de Ligne". 
**Abbreviated name**: *Peyre*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
