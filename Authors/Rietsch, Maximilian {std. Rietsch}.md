### Author

Rietsch, Maximilian (*fl*. 1882), French pharmacist and botanist; sometime professor at the school of Pharmacy and Medicine of Marseilles. (*Rietsch*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1703; CSP 11: 180; Kelly p. 192.
