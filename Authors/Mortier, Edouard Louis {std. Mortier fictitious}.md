### Author

Mortier, Edouard Louis (1801-1852), fictitious French naturalist said to have been a botanical explorer of South America. (*==Name in the standard form is not specified==.*).

#### Note

Fictitious botanist figuring in a commercial American encyclopaedia (Appleton's Cyclopedia of American Biography 4: 429. 1888), see Barnhart, J. H., J. New York Bot. Gard. 20: 176. 1919. Among the titles ascribed to him were *Historia plantarum circa Cayenne sponte crescentium (*1843), and *Prodromus florae brasilicae*(2 vols. 1849).

#### Eponymy

*Aquamortierella*R. W. Embree & H. Indoh (1967) and *Mortierella*E. Coemans (1863) commemorate Barthélémy Charles Joseph Dumortier (1797-1878), q.v. These eponyms were not mentioned in TL-2(1).
