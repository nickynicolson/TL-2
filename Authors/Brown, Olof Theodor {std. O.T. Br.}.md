### Author

Brown, Olof Theodor (1845-1893), Swedish telegraph operator in Göteborg and botanist. (*O.T. Br.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

KR p. 106 (b. 30 Jul 1845, Skåne, d. 1 Mai 1893, Göteborg).
