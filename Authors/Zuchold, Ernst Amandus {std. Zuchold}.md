### Author

Zuchold, Ernst Amandus (x-1867), German (Saxonian) bibliographer, bookseller and antiquarian at Leipzig. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Zuchold*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – *Letters* to D.F.L. von Schlechtendal in [[Collection HAL|HAL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2401; CSP 6: 520, 8: 1309; Herder p. 76, 423; Jackson p. 3; NI p. 256; Rehder 5: 942; Tucker 1: 760.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Brit. Mus. gen. cat. print. books 263: 930-931. 1966 (q.v. for further, non-botanical, publ.).
- Helm, J., Kulturpflanze 14: 354. 1966 (d. 9 Sep 1867).

#### Composite works

Editor, *Bibliotheca historico-naturalis* et physico-chemica, 1-17(1), 1851-1867.
