### Author

Scheit, Max (1858-1888), German (Sachsen-Weimar-Eisenach) botanist and high school teacher; Dr. phil. Jena 1883; teacher at Sonnenberg. (*Scheit*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 223; CSP 18: 500; Morren ed. 10: 24; Rehder 5: 765; Tucker 1: 628.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Bot. Centralbl. 37: 327. 1889 (d. 22 Dec 1888); Bot. Zeit. 47: 65. 1889 (id.); Leopoldina 25: 51. 1889 (d.).
- Scheit, M., Vita, *in* Dissertation, p. \[31\], see below (b. 3 Feb 1858).
