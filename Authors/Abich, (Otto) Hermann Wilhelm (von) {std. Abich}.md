### Author

Abich, \[Otto\] Hermann Wilhelm \[von\] (1806-1886), German palaeobiologist at Dorpat, St. Petersburg and Vienna. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Abich*

#### Collections

Unknown; at his death in Vienna.
