### Author

Åkermark, Sophia (née Areschoug) (1817-1882), Swedish cryptogamist, sister of J. E. Areschoug. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Åkermark*

#### Herbarium and types

Issued a series of exsiccatae: *Typ-Sammling af Skandinaviens Alger innehållande 100 arten*. (nos. 1-100, Göteborg 1870) sets at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection F|F]], [[Collection FH|FH]], [[Collection UPS|UPS]], [[Collection W|W]], [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 29; KR p. 786.
- Sayre, Mem. New York Bot. Gard. 19(1): 57. 1967.
