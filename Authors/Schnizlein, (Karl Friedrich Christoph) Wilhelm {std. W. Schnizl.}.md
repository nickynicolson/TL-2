### Author

Schnizlein, \[Karl Friedrich Christoph\] Wilhelm (1780-1856), German (Bavarian) botanist and magistrate; lived some time (ca. 1806) in Austria, later "Landgerichtsarzt" (law court physician) at Monheim and Erlangen; associate of E.G. von Steudel through the Esslinger Reiseverein; father of Adalbert Schnizlein. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Schnizl.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection ER|ER]] (via his son, q.v.; the herbarium numbered some 18.000 species); duplicates at [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.), [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection GJO|GJO]], [[Collection KIEL|KIEL]], [[Collection M|M]], [[Collection MW|MW]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]], [[Collection REG|REG]]. These collections are often attributed to A. Schnizlein, who, however, never lived in Austria. W. Schnizlein contributed to the collections distributed by the Esslinger Reiseverein.

#### Note

Schnizlein published *Über die neueste Ecklon'sche Sendung* getrockneter Cap-Pflanzen, und über die Leistungen des naturhistorischen Reisevereins zu Esslingen, Flora 12: 113-118. 1829 and contributed *Schoenus* to J.C. Sturm, Deutschi. Fl.
