### Author

Quehl, Leopold (1849-1922), German (Saxonian) botanist, writer on cacti; grandson of F.L. Jahn; employee in the postal service at Halle; from 1873 interested in cacti, maintaining a large collection of living plants as well as assembling an herbarium. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Quehl*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (Cactaceae, in part extant).
