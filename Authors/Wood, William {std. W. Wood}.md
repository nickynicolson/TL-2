### Author

Wood, William (1745-1808), British clergyman (nonconformist minister) at Debenham (Suffolk), Stamford (Lincolnshire) and Ipswich (Suffolk, 1770-1772), and Mill Hill Chapel, Leeds (1773-1808). (*W. Wood*).
