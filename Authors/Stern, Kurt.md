### Author

Stern, Kurt (1892-x), German (Silesian) botanist; studied law in Berlin and natural sciences in Breslau and München; Dr. phil. München 1914; student of K. v. Goebel. 
**Abbreviated name**: *K. Stern*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 326; Biol.-Dokum. 17: 8951-8952; Moebius p. 254, 297, 325.

#### Note

Stern's later work was in plant physiology, e.g. *Elektrophysiologie der Pflanzen*, Berlin (Verlag von Julius Springer) 1924. Oct., vii, 220 p. (Monogr. Gesammtgeb. Physiol. Pfl. Tiere 4, 1924).
