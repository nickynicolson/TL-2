### Author

Ramis y Ramis, Juan (1746-1819), Spanish (Balearic) botanist at Mahon, Minorca. (*Ramis y Ramis*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 125; BM 8: 1046; Colmeiro 1: cxcv; Colmeiro penins. p. 199; \[fix\]Kew 4: 398: SO 157a.

#### Biofile

- Marès, P. & G. Vigineix, Cat. pl. vasc. Baléares p. xi. 1880.

#### Eponymy

The source of *Ramisia* Glaziou ex Baillon (1887) could not be determined.
