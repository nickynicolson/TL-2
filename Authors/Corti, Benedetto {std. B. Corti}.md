### Author

Corti, Benedetto (1866-1907), Italian botanist, born and died at Como, studied Foraminiferae and Diatomaceae. (*B. Corti*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 383 (b. 22 Jun 1866, Como, d. 27 Mar 1907, Como); De Toni 4: xiii-xiv; DTS 1: 51; Quenstedt p. 92; Saccardo 1: 57, 2: 36.
