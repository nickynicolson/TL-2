### Author

Combes, Jacques-Ludomir (1824-1892), Franch pharmacist and botanist at Fumel, interested in botany and paleontology of the Haut-Agenais and Lot-et-Garonne. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Combes*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
