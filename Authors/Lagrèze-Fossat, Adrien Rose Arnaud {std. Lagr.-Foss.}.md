### Author

Lagrèze-Fossat, Adrien Rose Arnaud (1814-1874), French lawyer and botanist at Moissac. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lagr.-Foss.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection TLM|TLM]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 335; BL 2: 207, 677; CSP 3: 804, 12: 423; IH 2: 404; PR 4989-4990 (ed. 1: 5496); Rehder 5: 483; Tucker 1: 407.
- Astre, Bull. Soc. Hist. nat. Toulouse 101: 177. 1966.

#### Eponymy

*Lagrezia* Moquin-Tandon (1849).
