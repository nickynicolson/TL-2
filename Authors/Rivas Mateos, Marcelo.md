### Author

Rivas Mateos, Marcelo (1875-1931), Spanish botanist; professor of botany at the Fac. de Farmacia, Barcelona and Madrid. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Riv. Mat.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MAF|MAF]].
