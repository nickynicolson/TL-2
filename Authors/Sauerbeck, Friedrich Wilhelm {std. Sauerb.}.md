### Author

Sauerbeck, Friedrich Wilhelm (1801-1882), German (Baden) botanist; "Hofgerichtsrath" (law court official) at Freiburg i.B.; ultimately "Oberlandesgerichtsrath" in Karlsruhe. (*Sauerb.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Sauerbeck's own bryophyte herbarium was acquired by Jean Nicolas Boulay and is now at [[Collection LILLE|LILLE]]; the "Jaeger and Sauerbeck" herbarium is at [[Collection NY|NY]] (see under A. Jaeger); further Sauerbeck material is at [[Collection FI|FI]] and [[Collection H|H]].
