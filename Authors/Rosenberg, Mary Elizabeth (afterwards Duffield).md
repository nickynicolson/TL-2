### Author

Rosenberg, Mary Elizabeth (afterwards: Duffield) (1820-1914), British (English) flower painter of Bath, Somerset; married William Duffield 1850. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M. Rosenberg*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 178; BB p. 262; BM 4: 1732; Desmond p. 530 (d. 13 Jan 1914); DNB 49: 248; GH p. 74; Jackson p. 41; NI 1678-1679, see also 3: 57; Sotheby 659.
