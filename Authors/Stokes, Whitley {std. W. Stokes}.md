### Author

Stokes, Whitley (1763-1845), Irish physician and botanist (bryologist), MD Dublin 1793; lecturer in natural history, Dublin, Trinity College 1814 (1816?); professor of medicine, Dublin 1830-1843. friend of Dawson Turner. (*W. Stokes*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
