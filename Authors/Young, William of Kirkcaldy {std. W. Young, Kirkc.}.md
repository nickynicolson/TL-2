### Author

Young, William of Kirkcaldy (1865-1947), British botanist; sometime member of the staff of the Royal Botanic Garden, Edinburgh. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Young, Kirkc.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection STA|STA]]; further material at [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BJI 2: 300, 734; Desmond p. 682 (d. 16 Mar 1947).
