### Author

Brongniart, Alexandre (1770-1847), French palaeobiologist, director of the Sèvres china factory, father of Adolphe Brongniart. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Al. Brongn.*

#### Collections

Muséum d'Histoire naturelle, Paris.
