### Author

Schaffner, Wilhelm (later: J. Guillermo Schaffner) (x-1882), German (Hessen) plant collector and pharmacist; settled in Mexico 1856; collected extensively around Mexico, Orizaba and San Louis Potosi. (*W. Schaffn.*).
