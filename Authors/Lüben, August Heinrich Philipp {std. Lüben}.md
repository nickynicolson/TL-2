### Author

Lüben, August Heinrich Philipp (1804-1874), German botanist and high school teacher at Weissenfels (1822-1825), Dorf-Alsleben (1825-1829), Aschersleben (1829-1850), Merseburg (1850-1858), and Bremen (1858-1874). (*Lüben*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
