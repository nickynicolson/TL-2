### Author

Rau, Ambrosius (1784-1830), German botanist and mineralogist; professor of natural history, forestry and rural economy at the University of Würzburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Rau*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – The majority of the herbarium collections at Würzburg ([[Collection WB|WB]]) were destroyed in World War II.

#### Bibliography and biography

Bamhart 3: 130 (b. 7 Mar 1784, d. 26 Jan 1830); BM 4: 1650; Herder p. 342; Kew 4: 410; PR 7427, ed. 1: 8371; Rehder 2: 284; Tucker 1: 582-583.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Flora 2: 524. 1819 (visit Würzburg), 13: 256. 1830 (d. 26 Jan 1830).
- Poggendorff, J.C., Biogr.-lit. Handw.-Buch 2: 574. 1863 (bibl.).

#### Eponymy

*Rauia* C.G.D. Nees et C.F.P. Martius (1823); *Ravia* J.A. Schultes (1824, *orth. var.*).
