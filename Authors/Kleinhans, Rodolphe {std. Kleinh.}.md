### Author

Kleinhans, Rodolphe (1828-?), French bryologist. (*Kleinh.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 2: 993; Kew 3: 275; NI 1067; PR 4716.

### Publications

##### n.3720. Album des mousses des environs de Paris

**Title**
*Album des mousses des environs de Paris*... Paris (chez l'auteur... F. Savy...) 1869.
**Abbreviated title**: *Album mousses Paris*.

**Notes**
*Publ*.: in 30 parts 1863-1869 (specimen plate submitted to Soc. bot. Fr. 24 Apr 1863; livr. 1, 2 BSbF 10 Jul 1863, 3, 4 ib. 13 Nov 1863, 5 Jun 1864, 6 ib. 12 Feb 1864, 9-14, ib. 23 Jun 1865; terminated with 30th livr. Jul 1869). Each livraison consisted of one plate with lithographed text; p. \[i\], pl. 1-30, with text, uncol. liths. by author. *Copies*: BR, MICH.
