### Author

Stebler, Friedrich Gottlieb (1852-1935), Swiss (Bern) agronomist, botanist and folklore student; Dr. phil. Halle 1876; head of the Swiss Seed testing institute in Zürich. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stebler*

#### Herbarium and types

Further material in [[Collection C|C]], [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]].
*Exsiccatae*: *Schweizerische Gräser-Sammlung*, 250 nos., 5 Lief. (with A. Volkart).

#### Bibliography and biography

AG 2(1): 223; Barnhart 3: 319 (b. 11 Aug 1852); BJI 1: 56; BL 2: 566, 589, 719; BM 5: 1864, 2006; CSP 11: 478, 18: 917; Herder p. 394; Jackson p. 86; Rehder 5: 821.
