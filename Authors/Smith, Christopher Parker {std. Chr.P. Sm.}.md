### Author

Smith, Christopher Parker (1835-1892), British bryologist; at Brighton and Hassocks (Sussex). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Chr.P. Sm.*

#### Herbarium and types

His herbarium, which included that of Edward Jenner, is now unlocalized.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 290; BB p. 279; BM 4: 1943; CSP 8: 967, 11: 432; Desmond p. 566 (b. 13 Oct 1835, d. 15 Nov 1892).
