### Author

Ahrens, Ernst (*fl*. 1892-1895), German botanist and teacher at the Kön. Victoria Gymnasium in Burg, nr. Magdeburg, Prussia. (*Ahrens*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
