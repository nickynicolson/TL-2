### Author

Schiønning, Holger Ludvig (1868-1942), Danish mycologist; cand. chem. 1893; assistant at the Carlsberg Laboratory; specialist in the physiology of yeasts. (*Schiønning*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
