### Author

Cavillier, François Georges (1868-1953), Swiss botanist at the Burnat herbarium 1890-1920, and the Conservatoire botanique de Genève. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Cavill.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography
