### Author

Tejada, R. (1913), Guatemala botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Tejada*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material in [[Collection US|US]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 1: 147, 321.

#### Note

Nothing is known to us about this author except that there is some material in US from Guatemala collected by R.A. Tejada (251 specimens), and that Bull. Torrey Bot. Club 41: 67. 1914 and BL 1: 147, 321 attribute the following publication to this author.
