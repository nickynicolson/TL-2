### Author

Gelshorn, G. (fl. 1875), Alsatian school teacher and botanist, "Oberlehrer", Gymnasium in Zabern ca. 1874-1875. (*Gelshorn*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Biofile

- Charpin, A. & G.G. Aymonin, 1999. J. Bot. Soc. Bot. France 10: 56.
