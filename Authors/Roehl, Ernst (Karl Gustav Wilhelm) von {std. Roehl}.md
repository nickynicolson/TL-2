### Author

Roehl, Ernst \[Karl Gustav Wilhelm\] von (1825-1881), German (Silezian-born Rheinland) palaeobotanist and soldier; in the Prussian army 1843-1869, 1870-1871. (*Roehl*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

ADB 29: 55. 1889 (Gümbel); Andrews p. 321; Barnhart 3: 169 (b. 1 Mai 1825, d. 19 Sep 1881); Biol.-Dokum. 15: 7583; CSP 5: 258, 8: 770; Herder p. 352; NI 1663; PR 7699; Quenstedt p. 370.
