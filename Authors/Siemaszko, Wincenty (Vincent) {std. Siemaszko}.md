### Author

Siemaszko, Wincenty \[Vincent\] (1887-x), Polish plant pathologist and mycologist; Dr. phil. Cracow; professor of plant pathology at the Warsaw college of Agriculture, also director of the Phytopathological Institute Skierniewice. (*Siemaszko*).

#### Herbarium and types

*Fungi Bialowiezenses exsiccati*, Warsaw 1924-1925, cent. 1-2, sets at [[Collection CUP|CUP]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection FH|FH]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection PRC|PRC]]. – With A. Wróblewski, *Fungi polonici selecti exsiccati*. Dec. 1-2, nos. 1-20. 1933, set at [[Collection FH|FH]].
