### Author

Esenbeck, Ernst (1889-x), German botanist, born 9 Mai 1889 at Munich, visited primary school and Theresien-Gymnasium at Munich, studied at the Ludwig-Maximilians-Universität natural sciences, assistant of Hegi in 1910, from 1911 assistant at the institute for plant physiology. (*Esenbeck*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
