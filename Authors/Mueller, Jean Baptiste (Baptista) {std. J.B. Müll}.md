### Author

Mueller, Jean Baptiste \[Baptista\] (1806-1894), German pharmacist and botanist at Mesebach, Emmerich and Berlin (*J. B. Müll*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 524; BM 3: 1369; CSP 12: 525, 17: 406; Jackson p. 311; PR 6519-6520 (ed. 1: 7276-7277); Rehder 5: 599; Tucker 1: 505.
- Anon., Bonplandia 6: 49. 1858 ("fürstl. waldeckscher Medicinalrath... Apothekebesitzer zu Berlin; cogn. Leopoldina: Dieffenbach; Leopoldina 30: 158. 1894 (b. 16 Apr 1806 \[Mainz\], d. 18 Jun 1894 \[Berlin\]).
