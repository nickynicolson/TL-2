### Author

Taylor, Adam (*fl*. 1769), British gardener to J. Sutton, New Park, near Devises, Wilts. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ad. Tayl*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

##### n.13.806. A treatise on the Ananas

**Title**

Devises (printed by T. Burrough, for the author,...) 1769. Oct. (in fours).
