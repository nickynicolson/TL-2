### Author

Söderberg, Daniel Henric (1750-1781), Swedish physician and botanist; student in Uppsala (1767, with Linnaeus) and Lund (1772); Dr. med. Lund 1778; physician at Eskilstuna. (*Söderberg*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
