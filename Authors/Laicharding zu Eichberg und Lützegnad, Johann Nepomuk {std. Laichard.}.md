### Author

Laicharding (Laicharting) zu Eichberg und Lützegnad, Johann Nepomuk (1754-1797), Austrian botanist and entomologist; professor of natural history ("Gubernialsekretär") at Innsbruck University. (*Laichard.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection IBF|IBF]].
