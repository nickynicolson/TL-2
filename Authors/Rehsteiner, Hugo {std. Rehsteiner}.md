### Author

Rehsteiner, Hugo (1864-1947), Swiss botanist at St. Gallen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rehsteiner*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (Lack 1980).

#### Bibliography and biography
