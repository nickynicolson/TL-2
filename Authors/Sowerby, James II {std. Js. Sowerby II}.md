### Author

Sowerby, James II (1815-1834), British botanical artist, grandson of James Sowerby, son of J. de Carle Sowerby. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Js. Sowerby II*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BB p. 284; Desmond p. 575; NI 1876.
