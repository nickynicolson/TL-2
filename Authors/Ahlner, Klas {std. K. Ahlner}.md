### Author

Ahlner, Klas (1845-x), Swedish highschool teacher and botanist. (*K. Ahlner*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some algae at [[Collection B|B]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 29.
- Urban, Gesch. Berlin-Dahlem 262. 1917.
