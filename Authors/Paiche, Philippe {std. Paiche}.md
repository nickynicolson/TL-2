### Author

Paiche, Philippe (1842-1911), Swiss botanist at Genève; studied Potentilla, Hieracium and Rosa. (*Paiche*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]] (via herb. Boissier); some further material at [[Collection BR|BR]] and [[Collection E|E]]. – Letters at [[Collection G|G]].

AG 6(1): 251, 12(2): 732; Barnhart 3: 41; DTS 6(4): 182; Hegi 4(2): 960; IH 2: (in press); Rehder 2: 291.
