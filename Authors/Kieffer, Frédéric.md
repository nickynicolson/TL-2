### Author

Kieffer, Frédéric (182 7-192 7), French botanist of Alsatian origin at Marseille, director of the Petit Lycée. 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. Kieff.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
