### Author

Carpenter, William (1797-1874), British naturalist. (*Carpenter*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 1: 318; Jackson p. 19.

#### Eponymy

*Carpenteria* J. Torrey (1853) is dedicated to William Marbury Carpenter (1811-1848), American botanist.
