### Author

Tison, Adrien (*fl*. 1900-1909), French botanist at the University of Rennes; Dr. phil. Caen 1900. (*A. Tison*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

GR p. 353; Rehder 5: 852; Tucker 1: 692, 2: xxxviii.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Hedwigia 53: (235). 1913 ("Maître de conférences, Fac. Sci., Univ. Rennes); Österr. bot. Z. 63: 136. 1913 (app. Rennes).
