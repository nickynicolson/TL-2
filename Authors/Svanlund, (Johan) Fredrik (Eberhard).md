### Author

Svanlund, \[Johan\] Fredrik \[Eberhard\] (1832-1902), Swedish botanist; telegraph inspector at Karlskrona 1856-1892; later director of a technical school. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Svanlund*

[[Collection LD|LD]]. – Further material at [[Collection DAO|DAO]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection GB|GB]], [[Collection L|L]], [[Collection M|M]], [[Collection NA|NA]], [[Collection NMW|NMW]], [[Collection WRSL|WRSL]].
