### Author

Pepoon, Herman Silas (1860-1941), American botanist; head instructor in botany and agriculture, Lake View High School, Chicago, Ill. (*Pepoon*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection ILLS|ILLS]]; further material at [[Collection F|F]], [[Collection ILL|ILL]], [[Collection ISM|ISM]], [[Collection MICH|MICH]], [[Collection MSC|MSC]].
