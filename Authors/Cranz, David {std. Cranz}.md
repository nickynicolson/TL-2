### Author

Cranz, David (1723-1777), Moravian missionary of the Moravian brethren, born at Neugarten in Pommern, in Greenland 1761-1762, from 1766 preacher in Rixdorf near Berlin, 1771 in Gnadenfrei. (*Cranz*).
