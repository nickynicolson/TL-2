### Author

Diez, Rudolf (fl. 1887), German botanist, born at Karlsruhe, studied at the University of Freiburg im Breisgau. (*R. Diez*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 455; Rehder 1: 108; Tucker 1: 203.
