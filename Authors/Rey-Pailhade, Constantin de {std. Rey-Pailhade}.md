### Author

Rey-Pailhade, Constantin de (1844-1930), French pteridologist. (*Rey-Pailhade*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection MPU|MPU]] (herb. J. de Vichet).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 148; BL 2: 106, 165, 708; CSP 18: 157; Kew 4: 453.

#### Biofile
