### Author

Bobisut, O. (fl. 1904), Austrian botanist, student of G. Haberlandt, assistant at the Botanical Institute of the University of Graz. (*Bobisut*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Tucker 1: 89.
