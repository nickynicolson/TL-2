### Author

Grevel, Wilhelm (1869-\[after 1930\]), German botanist, born 8 Apr 1869 in Steele an der Ruhr (Rheinland), doctorate 1897 Heidelberg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Grevel*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
