### Author

Nonne, Johann Philipp (1729-1772), German botanist at Erfurt. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Nonne*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 398; BM 3: 1441; Dryander 3: 160, 396, 400; Frank 3(Anh.): 72; Hegi 5(3): 2205; Kew 4: 156; LS 37464; PR 6737-6738 (ed. 1: 7564-7566); Rehder 5: 624; SA 1: 263; TL-1/923; TL-2/1738.
- Meusel, J. G., Lex. deut. Schrifst. 10: 126. 1810 (bibl., b. 7 Feb 1729, d. 14 Mar 1772).
- Zuchold, E. A., Jahresb. nat. Ver. Halle 5: 44. 1853.

#### Eponymy

*Nonea* \[sic\] Medikus (1789) was probably named for him. *Nonnea* H. G. L. Reichenbach (1831, *orth. var.*).
