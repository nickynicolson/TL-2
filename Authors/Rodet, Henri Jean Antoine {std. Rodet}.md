### Author

Rodet, Henri Jean Antoine (1810-1875), French botanist; teacher at the veterinary college of Lyon; later at Toulouse. (*Rodet*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 167 (b. 2 Oct 1810, d. 24 Oct 1875); BM 4: 1716; Jackson p. 53, 203; PR 7693-7694, ed. 1: 8653; Rehder 5: 732.

#### Biofile

- Magnin, Bull. Soc. bot. Lyon 32: 16. 1907 (further refs.).

#### Eponymy

*Rodetia* Moquin-Tandon (1849).
