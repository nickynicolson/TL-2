### Author

Skepper, Edmund (1825-1867), British botanist and druggist at Harwich and Bury St. Edmunds. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Skepper*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BM|BM]]. – *Letters* also at [[Collection BM|BM]].
