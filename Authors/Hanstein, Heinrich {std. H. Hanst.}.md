### Author

Hanstein, Heinrich (1825-1871), German botanist and pharmacist. (*H. Hanst.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 2: 784; CSP 3: 172; PR 3766-3767; Rehder 5: 365.
- Hein, W. H. & H. D. Schwarz, Deut. Apoth.-Biogr. 1: 247-248. 1975.
