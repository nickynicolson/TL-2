### Author

Therry, Jean Joseph \[né Terri\] (1833-1888), French merchant and banker, self-taught botanist at Lyon; one of the founders of the Société botanique de Lyon. (*Therry*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LY|LY]]. – Dupl. fungi [[Collection PAD|PAD]]; *Cryptogames du Lyonnais* [[Collection FH|FH]], [[Collection H|H]]-NYL (Lich.), [[Collection PAD|PAD]]. – See D.H. Pfister, Mycotaxon 23: 123. 1985.

#### Bibliography and biography
