### Author

Detharding, Georg Gustav (1765-1838), German botanist at Rostock. (*Dethard.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection ROST|ROST]]; material also at [[Collection B|B]] and [[Collection L|L]].
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 357, 2: 160.
