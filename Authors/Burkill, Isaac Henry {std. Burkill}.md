### Author

Burkill, Isaac Henry (1870-1965), British botanist who resided in Malaya for many years. (*Burkill*).

#### Herbarium and types

Mainly [[Collection CAL|CAL]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection SING|SING]].
*Ref*.: IH 2: 108.
