### Author

Monroe, Charles Edwin (1857-1931), American botanist, LL.B. Univ. Mich. 1880; lawyer at Elyria (1880-1881), Washington, D.C. 1881-1884 and Madison, Wisconsin 1884-1930; at Oberlin 1930-1931. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Monroe*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MIL|MIL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 505; CSP 17: 322; IH 2: 552; Pennell, p. 614.
- Benke, H. C, Torreya 36: 117-123. 1936 (studies on the Monroe coll. of Aster at MIL; b. 28 Mar 1857, d. 12 Mai 1931; biogr. notice by A. F. Fuller on p. 117).
