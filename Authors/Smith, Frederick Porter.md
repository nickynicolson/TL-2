### Author

Smith, Frederick Porter (1833-1888), British medical missionary in Central China; MB Univ. London 1855; after his return to England medical practitioner at Shepton Mallett. 
**Abbreviated name**: *F.P. Sm.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
