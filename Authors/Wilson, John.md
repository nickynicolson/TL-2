### Author

Wilson, John (1696-1751), British shoemaker, baker and botanist at Kendal, Westmorland. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Wils.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 504; BB p. 330; BM 5: 2333; Desmond p. 667 (d. 15 Jul 1751); DNB 21: 577 (G.S. Boulger) (62: 106); Dryander 3: 25, 131; Henrey no. 1501; Jackson p. 232; PR 10307, ed. 1: 11281; Tucker 1: 749.

#### Biofile

- Baker, J.G., Bot. Trans. Yorkshire Natural. Union 1: 46; Nat. Hist. Trans. Northumberland Durham and Newcastle-upon-Tyne 14: 75-76. 1902.
- Davey, F.H., Fl. Cornwall xxxii. 1909.
- Pulteney, R., Hist. biogr. sketches Progr. Bot. England 2: 264-269. 1790.

#### Eponymy

*Wilsonia* R. Brown (1810). *Note*: The following names honor Prof. L.K. Wilson, American geologist: *Wilsonastrum* Jansonius (1962), *Wilsonia* R.M. Kosanke (1950), *Wilsonipites* S.K. Srivastava (1969), *Wilsonisporites* A. Kimyai (1966) and *Wilsonites* R.M. Kosanke (1959).
