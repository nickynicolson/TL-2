### Author

Stromeyer, Johann Friedrich (1750-1830), German (Hannover) physician and botanist; Dr. med. Göttingen 1772. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Stromeyer*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2037; Dryander 3: 216; PR 9011, ed. 1: 9966; Rehder 3: 698; TL-1/1274 (some refs. are to F. Stromeyer); Tucker 1: 671.

#### Biofile

- Pritzel, G.A., Linnaea 19(3): 463. 1846 (warns against confusion between F. and J.F. Stromeyer).
- Stromeyer, G.F.L., Erinnerungen eines deutschen Arztes ed. 2. 111-112. 1875 (repr. 1977).
