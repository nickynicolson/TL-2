### Author

Bouloumoy, Louis (x-1926), French jesuit priest and during 35 years professor at the French Faculty of Médecine of Beyrouth. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Boul.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
