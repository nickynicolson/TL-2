### Author

Uildriks, Frederike Johanna van (*fl*. 1898), Dutch popular writer on botany. (*Uildriks*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 414; Rehder 5: 867.
