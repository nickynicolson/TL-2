### Author

Clapp, Asahel (1792-1862), American botanist of New Albany, Indiana. (*A. Clapp*).

#### Herbarium and types

See C.C. Deam, *Flora of Indiana* 1940, p. 1116, material at [[Collection HH (sensu TL-2)|HH]] and [[Collection WAB|WAB]] (180).
