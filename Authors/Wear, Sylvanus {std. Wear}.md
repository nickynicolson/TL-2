### Author

Wear, Sylvanus (1858-1920), British field naturalist and flour miller; in Belfast from 1904. (*Wear*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BEL|BEL]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 466; BB p. 319; Desmond p. 647 (b. 21 Mai 1858, d. 13 Nov 1920).
