### Author

Schneider, Ferdinand (1834-1882), Swiss botanist and pharmacist in Basel. (*Fe. Schneid.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]]. – Letters at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 568, 715; BM 4: 1851; Jackson p. 344; Rehder 1: 436.
