### Author

Buchli, Matheus (1898-x), Swiss agronomist and botanist, assistant at the Eidg. Landwirtschaftl. Versuchsanstalt Zürich-Oerlikon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M. Buchli*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
