### Author

Stadler, Solomon (1842-1917), Swiss botanist and teacher; Dr. phil. Zürich 1886; rector of the girls high school in Winterthur 1872-1878, id. at Zürich 1878-1891, rector ib. 1891-1911. (*Stadler*).
