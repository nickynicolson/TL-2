### Author

Wall, George (1821-1894), British merchant, planter, newspaper editor and pteridologist; in Ceylon from 1846-1894; friend of G.H.K. Thwaites. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Wall*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]] (fern herb.); further material in [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection HH (sensu TL-2)|HH]], [[Collection LIV|LIV]], [[Collection PDA|PDA]].
