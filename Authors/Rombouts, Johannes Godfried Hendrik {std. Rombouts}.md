### Author

Rombouts, Johannes Godfried Hendrik (x-1889), Dutch botanist; Dr. med. Leiden 1843. (*Rombouts*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 435, 710; BM 4: 1726; Oudemans 2: 255; PR 7750; Rehder 1: 391; Tucker 1: 605.
