### Author

Maury, Paul Jean Baptiste (1858-1893), French botanist, in Mexico 1884-1893. (*Maury*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MEXU|MEXU]], [[Collection P|P]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 465 (b. 21 Dec 1858, d. 25 Dec 1893); BJI 1: 39; BL 1: 19, 309; BM 3: 1271; CSP 17: 107; IH 2: 517; Langman p. 489; Morrcn ed. 10, p. 54; MW p. 311; Tucker 1: 472.
- Anon., Bot. Centralbl. 60: 224. 1894 (d.); Bot. Not. 1895: 38 (d.); J. de Bot. 4: 60. 1890, 8: 288. 1894 (d.).
- Bescherelle, E., J. de Bot. 15: 380. 1901 (bryol. coll. from Mexico).
- Leon, N., Bibl. bot.-mexic. 186. 1895.

#### Eponymy

*Maurya* Patouillard (1898).
