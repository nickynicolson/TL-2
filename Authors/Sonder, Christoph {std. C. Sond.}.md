### Author

Sonder, Christoph (*fl*. 1890), German (Holstein) pharmacist and botanist; Dr. phil. Rostock 1890; pharmacist in Oldesloe, Schleswig-Holstein, nephew of O.W. Sonder. (*C. Sond.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
