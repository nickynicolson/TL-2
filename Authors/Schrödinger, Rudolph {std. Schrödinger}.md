### Author

Schrödinger, Rudolph (1857-1919), Austrian botanist. (*Schrödinger*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (extant; in herb. Zool.-bot. Ges. Wien).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 242 (b. 27 Jan 1857, d. 24 Dec 1919); BM 4: 1864; Hortus 3: 1203; Zander ed. 10, p. 714, ed. 11, p. 814.

#### Biofile

- Lack, H.W., Willdenowia 10: 81. 1980 (herb. mat. at B; extant).
- Schmid, B. & C. Thesing, Biol.-Kalend. 1: 309. 1914.
