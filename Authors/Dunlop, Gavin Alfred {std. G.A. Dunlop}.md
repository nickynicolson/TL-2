### Author

Dunlop, Gavin Alfred (1868-1933), British botanist, born at Nottingham 1868, curator of the Warrington Museum, Lancs., died 3 Apr 1933. (*G.A. Dunlop*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 251; BM 6: 284; Desmond p. 200.
