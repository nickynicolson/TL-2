### Author

Lauterer, Joseph (1848-1911), German physician; surgeon in the Franco-Prussian war; botanist and phytochemist; originally at Freiburg i.B., from Mar 1885 in Australia at Brisbane. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lauterer*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
