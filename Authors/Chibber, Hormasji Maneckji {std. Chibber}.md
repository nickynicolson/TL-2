### Author

Chibber, Hormasji Maneckji (1894-x), Agriculturist and botanist, some time with John Innes Institute and at agricultural College, Poona, India. (*Chibber*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 341; LS suppl. 4945.
