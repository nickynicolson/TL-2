### Author

Monckton, Horace Woollaston (1857-1931), British barrister, botanist and geologist in Berkshire; treasurer of the Linnean Society 1905-1930. (*Monckton*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection RNG|RNG]]; other material [[Collection OXF|OXF]].
