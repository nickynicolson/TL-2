### Author

Rossetti, Corrado (1866-?), Italian botanist; high school teacher of natural history and mathematics at Pisa (1891), in Seravezza (1896) and Querceta (1908). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Rossetti*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PI|PI]]; further material at [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.), [[Collection BI|BI]] and [[Collection GE|GE]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 181 (b. 7 Feb 1866); BL 2: 402, 710; CSP 18: 306; GR p. 538; LS 22545; Saccardo 1: 142, 2: 93; Urban-Berl. p. 389.

#### Biofile
