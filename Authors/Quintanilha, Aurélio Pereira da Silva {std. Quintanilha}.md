### Author

Quintanilha, Aurélio Pereira da Silva (1892-x), Portuguese botanist; Dr. phil. Coimbra 1926; at the Botanical Institute of Coimbra. (*Quintanilha*).

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection LMJ|LMJ]].
