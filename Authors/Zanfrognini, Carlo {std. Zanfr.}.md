### Author

Zanfrognini, Carlo (1866-?), Italian physician and lichenologist in Modena, sometime assistant at the botanical garden of Modena. (*Zanfr.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

CSP 19: 763; De Toni 4: lxv; GR p. 525, cat. p. 74; LS 29513-29516, 40797, suppl. 30694-30696; Saccardo 1: 175.
