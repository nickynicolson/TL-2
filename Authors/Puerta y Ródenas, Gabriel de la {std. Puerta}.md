### Author

Puerta y Ródenas, Gabriel de la (*fl*. 1891), Spanish botanist, professor of botany at the Universidad Central, Madrid. (*Puerta*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 2: 483, 705; Rehder 5: 693.
