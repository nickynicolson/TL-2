### Author

Deecke, Johannes Ernst Wilhelm (1862-1934), German geologist and palaeobiologist at Freiburg i. Br. (*Deecke*).

#### Collections

Freiburger geologisches Institut.

Andrews p. 253; Barnhart 1: 433; BM 1: 434, 6: 258; CSP 14: 521-522; Quenstedt p. 109.
