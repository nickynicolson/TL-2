### Author

Deflandre, Georges (-Victor) (1897-1973), French palaeontologist and algologist. (*Deflandre*).

#### Collections

Institut de Paléontologie, Muséum National d'Histoire naturelle, Centre scientifique Georges Deflandre (12.000 books, 15.000 preparations, various manuscripts)
