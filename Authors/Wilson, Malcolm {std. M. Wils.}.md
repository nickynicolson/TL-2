### Author

Wilson, Malcolm (1882-1960), British botanist; D.Sc. London 1911; demonstrator in botany, Imperial College London 1909; lecturer in mycology Edinburgh Univ. 1909, reader 1925-1951. 
**Abbreviated name**: *M. Wils.*
**Abbreviated name**: *M. Wils.*
