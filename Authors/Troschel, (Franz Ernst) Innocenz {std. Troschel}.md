### Author

Troschel, \[Franz Ernst\] Innocenz (1858-?), German (Berlin) high school teacher and botanist; studied in Berlin and Tübingen; Dr. phil. Berlin Univ. 1879. (*Troschel*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 402; Biol.-Dokum. 18: 9382; Jackson p. 90; Morren ed. 10, p. 7; Rehder 1: 125; TL-2/11.016; Tucker 1: 701, 3: 313.

#### Biofile

- Troschel, I., Unters. Mestom Dikot. Laubbäume \[24\]. 1879 (vita in dissertation; b. 2 Apr 1858 in Berlin, student of e.g. Ascherson, Eichler, Hofmeister, Kny und Schwendener).

#### Eponymy

*Troschelia* Klotzsch et Schomburgk (1843).
