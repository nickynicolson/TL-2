### Author

Riva, Domenico (ca. 1856-1895), Italian botanist and explorer of Africa with Schweinfurth (1892) and Ruspoli (1892-1893); botanical assistant at Bologna. (*Riva*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection FT|FT]]; some further material at [[Collection BR|BR]], [[Collection RO|RO]] (fide A. White & B.L. Sloane 1937), and [[Collection TO|TO]].
