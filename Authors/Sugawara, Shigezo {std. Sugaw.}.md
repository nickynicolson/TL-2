### Author

Sugawara, Shigezo (*fl*. 1937), Japanese botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Sugaw.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; some Sachalin material at [[Collection A|A]].

#### Bibliography and biography

MW p. 336, suppl. p. 336.

#### Biofile

- Frodin, D.G., Guide standard floras world no. 733. 1984.
- Verdoorn, F., ed., Chron. bot. 7: 362. 1943.

### Publications

##### n.13.391. Plants of Saghalien

**Title**
*Plants of Saghalien* a list of the vegetation-totality in the Saghalien. With the descriptions of ligneous plants, useful plants and naturalized plants... Toyohara, Saghalien 1937. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Pl. Saghalien*.

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 1937, p. \[i-vi\], 1-490, map. *Copies*: HH, US. – In Japanese with English title. No new taxa.

##### n.13.392. Illustrated flora of Saghalien

**Title**
*Illustrated flora of Saghalien* with descriptions and figures of Phanerogams and higher cryptogams indigenous to Saghalien... 1937-1940, s.l. 4 vols.
**Abbreviated title**: *Ill. fl. Saghalien*.

**Notes**

*3*: 1940, p. \[i-iii\], 1-10, \[971\]-1437, *pl. 444-656.* – Papaveraceae-Cornaceae.
*4*: 1940, p. \[i-iii\], 1-6, \[1439\]-1957, *pl. 657-892.* –Diapensiaceae-Asteraceae.
*Copies*: B-S, HH, M, NY, PH, USDA (t.p.'s copy NY lack the word "illustrated" on the t.p.'s).
