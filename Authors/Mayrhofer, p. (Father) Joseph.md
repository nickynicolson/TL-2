### Author

Mayrhofer, p. \[Father?\] Joseph (1831-1880), German botanist, "Conventual des Benedictinerstifter Weltenburg." 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Mayrhofer*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
