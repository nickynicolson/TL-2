### Author

Waldenburg, Ilse (1908-x), German (Berlin) botanist; studied natural sciences at Berlin and München; Dr. phil. Berlin 1934. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Waldenburg*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
