### Author

Drejer, Salomon Thomas Nicolai (1813-1842), Danish botanist at the veterinary school at Copenhagen, editor of *Flora danica* 1841-1842, died of snuff poisoning. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Drejer*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
