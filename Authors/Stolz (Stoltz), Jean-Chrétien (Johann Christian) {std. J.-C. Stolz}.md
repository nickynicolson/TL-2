### Author

Stolz \[Stoltz\], Jean-Chrétien \[Johann Christian\] (1764-1828), Alsatian botanist; high school teacher at Strasbourg ca. 1798-1828. (*J.-C. Stolz*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
