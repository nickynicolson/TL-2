### Author

Lachenal, Werner de (1736-1800), Swiss botanist; Dr. med. Basel 1759; professor of anatomy and botany at Basel (1776-1800) 
**Abbreviated name**: *Lachen.*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BAS|BAS]]; some other material at [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection LIV|LIV]].
*Ref*.: Binz, A., Verh. naturf. Ges. Basel 19(3): 148-150. 1908.

#### Bibliography and biography

Backer p. 310; Barnhart 2: 332; BM 3: 1042 (Wernerus de LaChenal); Dryander3: 80, 151, 5: 313; IH 1(ed. 6): 361, 2: 402; PR 4968-4969; Rehder 5: 481; Tucker 1: 406.
- Christ, H., Verh. naturf. Ges. Basel 29: 1-59. 1918 (corr. with Haller).
- Kirschleger, F., Fl. Alsace 2: xlii. 1857.

#### Eponymy

*Lachenalia* J.F. \[sic\] Jacquin (1780-1784).
