### Author

Lauth, Thomas (1758-1826), Alsatian botanist and physician; Dr. med. Strasbourg 1781; professor of anatomy at Strasbourg. (*Lauth*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 351; CSP 3: 888; Dawson p. 523; Dryander 3: 335; Frank 3(Anh.): 55; Hortus 3: 1198 ("Lauth"); PR 5092 (ed. 1: 5589-5590); Zander ed. 10, p. 683. Z., Dict. sci. méd. 5: 545. 1822.
