### Author

Thiele, Friedrich-Leopold (x-1841), German (prob. Prussian) botanist; assistant at the Berlin herbarium 1838-1841. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Thiele*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
*Exsiccatae*: *Laub-Moose der Mittelmark*, 1832, 170 nos., see below. Set of specimens: [[Collection FH|FH]], but see also copies of text.

#### Bibliography and biography

Urban-Berl. p. 315; Zep-Tim. p. 108.

#### Biofile

- Ascherson, P., Fl. Prov. Brandenburg 1: 15. 1864 (d. 1841).

#### Eponymy

*Thieleodoxa* Chamisso (1834).
