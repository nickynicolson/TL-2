### Author

Glocker, Ernst Friedrich von ("Constantino") (1793-1858), German mineralogist and palaeobiologist at Stuttgart. (*Glocker*).

#### Collections

Herbarium material at B, BM, K, LE, MANCH, MO, NY, OXF, S, US.
*Ref*.: IH 2: 227.
