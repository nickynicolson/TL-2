### Author

Weyl, Carl Friedrich (1792-1872), German (East-Prussian) botanist and high school teacher; at the Friedrichs-Kolleg in Königsberg i. Pr. 1814, at the Rastenburger Gymnasium from 1817-1858. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Weyl*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
