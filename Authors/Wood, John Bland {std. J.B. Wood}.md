### Author

Wood, John Bland (1813-1890), British physician and bryologist; studied at Dublin, Edinburgh and London; practicing physician at Broughton nr Manchester; ultimately in retirement at Withington. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J.B. Wood*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MANCH|MANCH]] (bryoph.); Further material at [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection HFX|HFX]] (1400, see Kent & Allen 1984), [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection NMW|NMW]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]], [[Collection SWN|SWN]], [[Collection WAR|WAR]]. – *Letters* at [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]]. – For his correspondence see Wm. A. Lee (1920).
