### Author

Auer, Auvo Vilho (1909-1945), Finnish botanist in Turku, studied ib. 1928, Mag. phil. 1935, highschool teacher. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Auer*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection H|H]], [[Collection TUR|TUR]].
