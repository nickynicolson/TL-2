### Author

Sterler, Alois (1787-1831), German (Bavarian) botanist. (*Sterler*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 5: 2017; GF p. 77; Jackson p. 226; NI 1894; PR 8949-8950, ed. 1: 9909-9910; Rehder 5: 824; Tucker 1: 668.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Faunus 1: 51. 1832 (obit. not., d. 15 Dec 1831).
