### Author

Koltz, Jean Pierre Joseph (1827-1907), Belgian/Luxemburg botanist; forester in Luxemburg, founder of the société botanique du G. D. de Luxemburg. (*Koltz*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LUX|LUX]]; further material at [[Collection BR|BR]].
