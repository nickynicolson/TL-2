### Author

Dalton, Henry G. (fl. 1855), British explorer and botanist in British Guyana. (*H.G. Dalton*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BL 1: 243; BM 1: 417; Jackson p. 375; Kew 2: 7; Rehder 3: 64; Tucker 1: 184.

London (Longman, Brown, Green and Longmans) 1855. Oct.
