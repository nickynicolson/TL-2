### Author

Metz, Sister Mary Clare (1907-x), American botanist and nun at the Congregation of the Sisters of Divine Providence San Antonio, Texas; Ph. D. Cathol. Univ. Amer. 1934; later at Our Lady of the Lake College. (*Metz*).
