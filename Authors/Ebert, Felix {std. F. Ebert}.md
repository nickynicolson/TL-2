### Author

Ebert, Felix (fl. 1907), Swiss pharmacologist working at "Pharmazeutisches Institut des Eidgenössischen Polytechnikums" in Zürich, Ph.D. 1907 also in Zürich. (*F. Ebert*).

[[Collection P|P]] (Pter.), [[Collection Z|Z]].
