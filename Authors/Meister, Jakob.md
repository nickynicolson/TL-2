### Author

Meister, Jakob (1850-1927), Swiss botanist and geologist; high school teacher at Zürich 1871-1880, at Schaffhausen 1880-1926. 
**Abbreviated name**: *J. Meister*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection E|E]] and [[Collection GB|GB]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 472; BL 2: 580, 690; BM 3: 1282; CSP 17: 141; IH 2: 523; Rehder 5: 563.
