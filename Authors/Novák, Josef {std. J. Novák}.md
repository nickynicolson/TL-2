### Author

Novák, Josef (1846-1917), Czech botanist and high school teacher at Nemecký Brod (Deutschbrod, Havlíckåv Brod) 1874-1886, later at Jicín (Jitschin). (*J. Novák*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PR|PR]].
