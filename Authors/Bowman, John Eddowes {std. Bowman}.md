### Author

Bowman, John Eddowes (1785-1841), British banker and botanist at Wrexham and Manchester. (*Bowman*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection K|K]]; mosses at [[Collection OXF|OXF]].
*Ref*.: BB p. 41; IH 2: 91.
- Clokie, Account herbaria Oxford 137. 1964.
