### Author

Miltitz, Friedrich Joseph Franz Xaver von (x-1840), German botanical bibliographer at Dresden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Miltitz*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1317; Barnhart 2: 494; Herder p. 75; Jackson p. 2; Kew 4: 10; LS 17903a; PR 6247 (ed. 1: 6950); Rehder 1: 2; SO 3647.
- Anon., Bot. Not. 1840: 211 (d. 6 Mar 1840); Flora 23: 543. 1840 (d. 6 Mar 1840).

#### Eponymy

*Miltitzia* Alph. de Candolle (1845).
