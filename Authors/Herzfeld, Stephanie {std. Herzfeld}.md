### Author

Herzfeld, Stephanie (1868-1930), Austrian high school teacher and botanist; Dr. phil. Wien 1923; for many years honorary collaborator at WU; pupil of Wettstein. (*Herzfeld*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
