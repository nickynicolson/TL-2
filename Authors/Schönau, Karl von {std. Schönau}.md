### Author

Schönau, Karl von (1885-1944), German (Bavarian) botanist; Dr. phil. München 1910; botanical assistant at München 1910-1913; curator for cryptogams at the Botanische Staatssammlungen 1913; head curator 1928. (*Schönau*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection M|M]] (see Hertel 1980, p. 431).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 236 (b. 22 Oct 1885, d. 14 Jan 1944?); GR p. 135 (d. 14 Jan 1944); LS suppl. 7970, 24848.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Hedwigia 57: (76)-(77). 10 Sep 1915 (app. München); Nat. Nov. 37: 264. 1915 (app. "Kustos" München).
- Hertel, H., Mitt. Bot. München 16: 431. 1980 (herb.; 5000 lichens).
