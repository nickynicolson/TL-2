### Author

Shope, Paul Franklin (1894-x), American mycologist; MS Univ. Colorado 1925; Dr. phil. Washington Univ. 1931; instructor, subsequently professor of botany at the University of Colorado 1923-1943. (*Shope*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection COLO|COLO]]; other material [[Collection MICH|MICH]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 273 (b. 3 Feb 1894); Ewan ed. 1: 134, 302, 304, ed. 2: 201-202; Stevenson p. 1258.

- Shope, P.F., Mycologia 21: 292-296. 1929 (history of mycological collectors in Colorado); Biol. News-Letter Univ. Colorado 6(2): 73. 1933 (on mycol. herb. COLO).
