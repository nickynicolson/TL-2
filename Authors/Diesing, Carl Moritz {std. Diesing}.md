### Author

Diesing, Carl Moritz (1800-1867), Austrian helminthologist, pupil of Jacquin Jr., collaborator of Endlicher, curator of the Vienna Zoological Museum. (*Diesing*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection W|W]], other material at [[Collection HBG|HBG]].
