### Author

Mouton, Victor (*fl*. 1875-1901), Belgian mycologist at Liège. (*V. Mouton*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BR|BR]] (rd. 1922); dupl. [[Collection PAD|PAD]] (Saccardo) and [[Collection GENT|GENT]]. – Mouton contributed also to C. Roumeguère, *Fungi selecti exsiccati*cent. 49, 1889. – A mss *Catalogue de mon herbier de Champignons*,Janvier 1897, and a mss *Liste des Ascomycètes*observées aux environs de Liège, 1 Janvier 1901, are at [[Collection BR|BR]]. (see also Bull. Soc. Bot. Belg. 1886: 137-162. *1 pl.*).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 521; CSP 17: 388; GR, cat. p. 68; Hawksworth p. 184; IH 2: 565(?); LS 18391-18395; Stevenson p. 1252.
- Penzig, O. & P. A. Saccardo, Malpighia 15: 221. 1901 (epon.).
- Rammeloo, J., Bull. Jard. bot. natl. Belg. 48: 221-230. 1978 (descr. fungus coll. \[incl. list types\] Mouton at BR; no biogr. details; bibl.).

#### Eponymy
