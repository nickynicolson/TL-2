### Author

Noehden, Heinrich Adolph \[August\] (1775-1804), German botanist at Göttingen. (*Noehd.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown; no material at [[Collection GOET|GOET]] (inf. G. Wagenitz).

#### Note

Noehden's brother Georg Heinrich (*G. Noehden*) (1770-1826), also born in Göttingen, settled in England 1793, L.L.D. Cantab. 1796, wrote on *Citrus*(Trans. Hort. Soc. London 3: 1-19. 1822) and contributed to Sowerby and Smith, English botany.
