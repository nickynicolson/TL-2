### Author

Van Geert, Auguste \[August\] (1888-1938), Belgian horticulturist at Gent, specialist on Azaleas. (*Van Geert*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Hortus 3: 1206; Zander ed. 10, p. 727, ed. 11, p. 829.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Cat. gen. livr. impr. Bibl. natl. 201: 758. 1971.
- O.K., Rev. Hortic. belg. étrang. 12: 280. 1886 (obit. not.).

#### Composite works

Co-editor, *Revue de l'horticulture belge et étrangère*,... 1 (-27), 1875(-1900), Gand (Gent).
