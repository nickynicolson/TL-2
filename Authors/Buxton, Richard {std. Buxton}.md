### Author

Buxton, Richard (1786-1865), British shoemaker, newsman (fide BB) and botanist at Manchester. (*Buxton*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection OXF|OXF]].
*Ref*.: Kent, Brit, herbaria 47. 1957.
- Clokie, Account herbaria Oxford 142. 1964.
