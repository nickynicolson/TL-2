### Author

Ortmann, Anton (1801-1861), German-Bohemian botanist and pharmacist at Ellbogen and Carlsbad; 1857-1861 burgomaster of Ellbogen. (*Ant. Ortmann*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection PR|PR]]; some further material at [[Collection MW|MW]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 33; BM 3: 1479; CSP 4: 701-702; Futák-Domin p. 447; GR p. 456; Klášterský p. 139; Maiwald p. 136, 148, 167, 168, 187; PR 6862-6864; Rehder 5: 637; Tucker 1: 531.
- Anon., Bot. Zeit. 20: 96. 1862; Lotos 11: 240. 1861 (d.); Österr. bot. Z. 12: 52. 1862 (d. 25 Nov 1861).
- Dilg, P. & W. Ilg, Deut. Apoth. Zeit. 118(7): 251. 1978 (member Regensb. bot. Ges.).
- Opiz, P. M., Flora 17: 592. 1834 (epon.).

#### Eponymy

*Ortmannia*Opiz (1834).

#### Note

We failed to locate a copy of his "Die Flora Karlsbads und seiner Umgegend" Stuttgart 1838. A copy was received by the Regensb. bot. Ges. on 1 Oct 1838 (Flora 21: 771. 1838).
