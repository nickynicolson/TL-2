### Author

Clauson, Th. (1817-1860), French colonial settler in Algeria, botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Clauson*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection P|P]] and [[Collection P|P]]-CO. Published a *Herbarium fontanesianum normale*, 1 century, Jan 1861 (Flora 44: 458, 480. 1861).
*Ref*.: IH 2: 129.
