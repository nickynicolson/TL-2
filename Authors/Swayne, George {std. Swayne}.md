### Author

Swayne, George (1746-1827), British botanist and clergyman; vicar at Pucklechurch, Glos. 1772; rector at Dyrham 1806. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Swayne*

#### Herbarium and types

For exsiccatae *Gramina pascua* see below.
