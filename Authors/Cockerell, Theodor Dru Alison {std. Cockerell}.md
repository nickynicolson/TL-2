### Author

Cockerell, Theodor Dru Alison (1866-1948), British born American naturalist. (*Cockerell*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection COLO|COLO]] (fossils), [[Collection US|US]] (inf. W. A. Weber).
*Ref*.: IH 2: 132.
