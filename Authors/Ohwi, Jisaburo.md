### Author

Ohwi, Jisaburo (1905-1977), Japanese botanist; Ph.D. Kyoto Univ. 1930; lecturer at Kyoto 1936-1943; at Buitenzorg 1943-1945; from 1946 at the Science Museum, Tokyo. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ohwi*
