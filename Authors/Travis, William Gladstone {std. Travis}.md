### Author

Travis, William Gladstone (1877-1958), British floristic botanist and lichenologist; employed by a firm of patent agents until 1937. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Travis*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection LIV|LIV]]; other material in [[Collection DBN|DBN]], [[Collection NMW|NMW]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 399; BL 2: 298, 314, 725; Clokie p. 256; Desmond p. 617 (b. 29 Apr 1877, d. 29 Mar 1958); GR p. 383, cat. p. 72.
