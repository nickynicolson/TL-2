### Author

Llenas y Fernández, Manuel (fl. 1900) Spanish lichenologist; originally at Madrid; later at Barcelona. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Llenas*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BC|BC]] (350 Lich.)

#### Bibliography and biography
