### Author

Lloyd, George N. (1804-1889), British botanist at Edinburgh. (*G. N. Lloyd*).

#### Herbarium and types

1250 sheets at [[Collection K|K]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 393; BB p. 190; BM 3: 1159; Desmond p. 390; IH 2: 453; Jackson p. 9; Kew 3: 477; PR 5545 (ed. 1: 6192); Rehder 5: 518; SO 831.
- Anon., Gard. Chron. 1923(1): 329.
- C. V. B. M., Bull. misc. Inf. Kew 1923: 189-190 (herb, at K).

#### Eponymy

*Lloydia* Salisbury ex H. G. L. Reichenbach (1830, *nom. cons.*) is dedicated to Edward Lloyd (or Lluyd, or Llhwyd) (1660-1709), Welsh antiquary and student of the flora of his country.
