### Author

\* Arendt, Johann Josef Franz (fl. 1828-1840), German (Hannoveranian) botanist at Osnabrück, member of the Regensburgische Botanische Gesellschaft (from 1837). 
**Abbreviated name**: *Arendt*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
