### Author

Martelli, Niccolo \[Martellius Nicolaus\] (x-1829), Italian professor of botany at Rome. (*N. Martelli*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

HU 629; Jackson p. 320; Kew 3: 599; Plesch p. 326; PR 5841 (ed. 1: 6545); Saccardo 1: 104; TL 1: 122; TL-2/1: 634; Tucker 1: 463.
- Anon., Flora 12: 240. 1829.
- Gager, C. S., Brooklyn Bot. Gard. Rec. 27: 287. 1938 (dir. of bot. gard. Roma 1777-1805).
- Geremicca, M., Bull. Orto bot. Napoli 3: 65. 1913.

#### Composite works

Co-author (with L. and C. Sabbati) of volumes 2-7 of Bonelli, *Hortus romanus*(TL-2/1: 634). *Further copies*: FI, FI-BN.

#### Eponymy

The derivation of *Martella*Endlicher (1836) and *Martella*Adanson ex Kuntze (1898) could not be established.
