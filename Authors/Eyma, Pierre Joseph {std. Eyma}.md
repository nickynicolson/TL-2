### Author

Eyma, Pierre Joseph (1903-1945), Dutch botanist, educated at Utrecht. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Eyma*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BO|BO]] (collections from Indonesia), Dutch material at [[Collection L|L]], European collections at [[Collection U|U]].
