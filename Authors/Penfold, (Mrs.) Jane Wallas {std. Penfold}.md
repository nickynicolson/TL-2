### Author

Penfold, \[Mrs.\] Jane Wallas (*fl*. 1820-1850), British botanical artist and naturalist; lived in Madeira. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Penfold*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 65; BB p. 240; BM 4: 1542; Desmond p. 487; Herder p. 218; Jackson p. 352; Kew 4: 266; NI 1503, see also 1: 122; PR 7030 (ed. 1: 7858); Rehder 1: 497.
