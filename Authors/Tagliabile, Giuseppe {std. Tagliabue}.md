### Author

Tagliabile, Giuseppe (*fl*. 1816), Italian botanist; director of a garden at Litta, Lainate, Lombardy. (*Tagliabue*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknonw. – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

PR 9096, ed. 1: 10042; Rehder 2: 89, 3: 378; Saccardo 1: 159; Zander ed. 10, p. 723, ed. 11, p. 824.

### Publications

##### n.13.659. Storia e descrizione della Littaea geminiflora

**Title**
*Storia e descrizione della Littaea geminiflora* lettera al Sig. Giuseppe Acerbi del Sig. Giuseppe Tagliabue... Milano (Per Giovanni Pirotta) 1816. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Stor. Littaea geminifl.*

**Notes**
*Authorship*: PR notes that L.V.F. Henckel von Donnersmarck assured him that the actual author of this letter was Giovanni de Brignoli di Brunnhoff (1774-1857) at Modena.
*Publ*.: 1816, p. \[1\]-14. *1 pl*. (uncol. copp.). *Copies*: B-S, G, HH. – Reprinted from Bibl. Ital. 1816(1): 100-111. *1 pl*. 1816.
