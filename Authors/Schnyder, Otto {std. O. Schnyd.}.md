### Author

Schnyder, Otto (*fl*. 1878), Argentine botanist; professor of botany at the University of Buenos Aires. (*O. Schnyd.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – [[Collection LZ|LZ]] (destr.) listed some material from "O. Schnyder"; some bryophytes and phanerogams were at [[Collection B|B]] (mainly destr.).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 236; BM 4: 1854; CSP 11: 334, 12: 662; Jackson p. 376; Morren ed. 10, p. 142; Rehder 5: 775; Urban-Berl. p. 313, 393.
