### Author

Burgeff, Hans Edmund Nicola (1883-x), German botanist, professor of botany in Halle, München, Göttingen and Würzburg. (*Burgeff*).

#### Herbarium and types

Material at [[Collection M|M]] and [[Collection WB|WB]].
