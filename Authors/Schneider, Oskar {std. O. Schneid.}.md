### Author

Schneider, Oskar (1841-1903), German (Saxonian) naturalist, traveller and teacher; Dr. phil. Leipzig 1866; studied at Leipzig 1860-1864; private teacher at Salzburg 1864-1866; teacher at the "Böttger'schen Institut", Dresden 1866-1867; private teacher in Egypt 1867-1869; teacher at the Freemason Institute, Dresden 1869-1872; teacher with the title professor at the Annen-Realgymnasium Dresden 1872-1893. (*O. Schneid.*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
