### Author

Cramer, Carl Eduard (1831-1901), Swiss algologist, professor of botany at Zürich. (*C. Cramer*).

#### Herbarium and types

Algae at [[Collection ZT|ZT]] (Koster); Grummann cites herbarium at [[Collection Z|Z]].
*Ref*.: GR p. 630.
- Koster, Taxon 18: 550. 1969.
