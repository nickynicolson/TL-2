### Author

Schaffranek, A. (*fl*. 1883-1894), American botanist at Palatka, Florida 1883-1894; later at St. Charles, Missouri. (*Schaffranek*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 221; BM 4: 1824; Rehder 1: 319.
