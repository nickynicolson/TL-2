### Author

Eysenhardt, Carl (Karl) Wilhelm (1794-1825), German physician, zoologist and botanist, born 21 Jan 1794 at Berlin, became professor extraordinarius at Königsberg in 1822, died 25 Dec 1825. (*Eysenh.*).
