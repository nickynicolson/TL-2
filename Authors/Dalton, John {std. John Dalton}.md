### Author

Dalton, John (1766-1844), British chemist and botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *John Dalton*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MCR|MCR]] (destroyed 1940), [[Collection E|E]].
*Ref*.: Adamson and Crabtree, Mem. Proc. Manchester lit. phil. soc. 63(1): 1-46. 1919 (also J. Bot. 57: 293-294. 1919).
- Kent, Brit. herbaria 50. 1957.
- Sims, J. Soc. Bibl. nat. Hist. 5(2): 117-120. 1969.
- Wood, J. Soc. Bibl. nat. Hist. 5(4): 270-271. 1970.

#### Bibliography and biography

BB p. 82; CSP 2: 133-135; DNB 13: 428.
- Dallman and Wood, Trans. Liverpool bot. Soc. 1: 67. 1909 (q.v. for earlier biographical accounts).

#### Eponymy

*Daltonia* W. J. Hooker & T. Taylor (1818) is dedicated to James Dalton (1764-1843), q.v.
