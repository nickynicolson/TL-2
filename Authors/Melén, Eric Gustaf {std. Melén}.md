### Author

Melén, Eric Gustaf (1801-1828), Swedish physician and botanist; student of C. P. Thunberg; Dr. med. Upsal. 1826; hospital physician at Kalmar 1826-1828 (*Melén*).

#### Herbarium and types

Types, if any, in [[Collection UPS|UPS]] (Thunberg herb.).

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 172; BM 3: 1283; KR p. 499 (b. 6 Oct 1850, d. 12 Feb 1919); PR 6073 (ed. 1, 6762).
