### Author

Puihn, Johann Georg (x-1793), German botanist and physician; regional physician at Kulmbach. (*Puihn*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1621; Dryander 3: 542; PR 7363 (ed. 1: 8249); Rehder 3: 260.
