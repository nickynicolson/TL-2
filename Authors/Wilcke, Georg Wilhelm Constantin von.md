### Author

Wilcke, Georg Wilhelm Constantin von (*fl*. 1788), German botanist. 
**Abbreviated name**: *G. Wilcke*

#### Herbarium and types

Some material at [[Collection SBT|SBT]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Herder p. 413; PR 10266, ed. 1: 11241; Rehder 1: 370, 4: 81; Tucker 1: 745.

#### Eponymy

The derivations of *Wilckea* Scopoli (1777) and *Wilckia* Scopoli (1777, *nom. rej.*) were not given.
