### Author

Pierce, Newton Barris (1856-1916), American phytopathologist for the U.S. government for the Pacific coast region 1889-1906; later private collector and breeder of rare plants at Santa Ana, Calif. (*N. Pierce*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection BPI|BPI]].
