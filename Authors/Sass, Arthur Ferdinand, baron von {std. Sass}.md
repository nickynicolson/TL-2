### Author

Sass, Arthur Ferdinand, baron von (1837-1870), Estonian (Baltic German) agronomist, land-owner, naturalist and botanist on the Island of Oesel (Saare, Estonia). (*Sass*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown. – Letters to D.F.L. von Schlechtendal at [[Collection HAL|HAL]].

BM 4: 1809; CSP 5: 410, 8: 835-836; Herder p. 201; Lipschitz 417; LS 23709-23710; PR 8043; Rehder 1: 362; TR 1231.
