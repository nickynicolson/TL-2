### Author

Schulze, \[Carolus Otto\] Rudolfus (1870-?), German (Berlin) botanist; studied at the Berlin University; Dr. phil. ib. 1893 as student of Adolf Engler. (*R. Schulze*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BJI 1: 52.
