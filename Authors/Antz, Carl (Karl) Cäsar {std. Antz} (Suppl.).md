### Author

\* Antz, Carl \[Karl\] Cäsar (1805-1859), German (Mosel) military physician and botanist ultimately "Bataillonsarzt beim 2. Jägerbataillon", Greifswald. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Antz*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
