### Author

Vachell, Eleanor (1879-1948), British botanist at Cardiff, student of the flora of Glamorgan. (*Vachell*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection NMW|NMW]] (includes the collections made by her father, Charles Tanfield V., 1848-1914). Other material at [[Collection BM|BM]], [[Collection GL|GL]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]].

#### Eponymy

*Vachellia* R. Wight et Arnott (1834) honors the Rev. G.H. Vachell who collected plants in China.
