### Author

Mayer, Adolf Theodor (1871-1952), German pharmacist and botanist at Tübingen. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ad. T. Mayer*

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection STU|STU]].

#### Biography and bibliography

Lehmann, A., Schwäbische Apotheker 64, 86-89, 93, 104, 202. 1951.
