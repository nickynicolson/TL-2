### Author

Fellerer, Carl (fl. 1892), German botanist, born in Freising, awarded a prize for his published thesis. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Fellerer*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 1: 533; Kew 2: 249; Langman p. 277; Rehder 2: 583.
