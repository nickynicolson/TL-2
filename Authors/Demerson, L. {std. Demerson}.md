### Author

Demerson, L. (17..-18..), French botanist with L. Demerson being a pseudonym or a later name of L(ouis) Hanin \[TL-2/2: 45\], born in Metz, "chevalier de l'ordre royal de la Légion-d'Honneur". 
**Abbreviated name**: *Demerson*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 1: 439; MD p. 84-86; Plesch p. 195, PR 2153, ed. 1: 2434; Rehder 1: 85, 3: 582.

#### Note

Main source for L. Demerson is by W.D. Margadant, *Early bryological literature*, Meded. Bot. Mus. Herb. Rijksuniv. Utrecht no. 283. Jun 1968.
