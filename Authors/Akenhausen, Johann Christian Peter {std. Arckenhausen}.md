### Author

Ckenhausen, Johann Christian Peter (1784-1855), German (Goslar, Freie Reichsstadt, later Prussian) botanical artist, working for C.L. Blume 1829-1832. (*Arckenhausen*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
