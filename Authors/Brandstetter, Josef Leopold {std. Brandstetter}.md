### Author

Brandstetter, Josef Leopold (1831-1924), Swiss teacher ("Erziehungsrat") and botanist at the "Höhern Lehranstalt in Luzern", Switzerland. (*Brandstetter*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BJI 2: 557-558, 633; Tucker 1: 107.
