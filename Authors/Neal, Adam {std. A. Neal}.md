### Author

Neal, Adam (*fl. 1770's*), British gardener to J. Blackburne at Warrington. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Neal*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 3: 1404; Desmond p. 460 Neale); Dryander 3: 99; Henrcy 2: 274, 643, 3: 96; Jackson p. 415; PR 6622 (ed. 1: 7393); Rehder 1: 53, 5: 610; Tucker 1: 512.
- Johnson, G. W., Hist. Engl, gardening 238. 1829.

### Publications

##### n.6661. A catalogue of the plants in the garden of John Blackburne

**Title**
*A catalogue of the plants in the garden of John Blackburne*, Esq. at Oxford, Lancashire. Alphabetically arranged according to the Linnaean system. By Adam Neal, Gardener. Warrington (printed by William Eyres) 1779. Oct. (in fours).
**Abbreviated title**: *Cat. gard. Blackburne*.

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 1779 (p.iii: 1 Feb 1779), p. \[i-iv\], \[1\]-72. *Copy*: BR. –Henrey 1162 cites copies at BM-Bloomsbury and OXF. – See also R. Weston, Suppl. English Fl. 99-114. 1780.
