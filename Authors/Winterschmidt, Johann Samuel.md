### Author

Winterschmidt, Johann Samuel (1760-1824 \[1824-1829?\]), German (Bavarian) botanical artist at Nürnberg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Winterschmidt*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Jackson p. 301; NI see indexes; PR 10340, ed. 1: 11312; Rehder 5: 927; TL-2/10.835.

### Publications

##### n.18.030. Nürnbergische Flora

**Title**
*Nürnbergische Flora* oder natürliche Abbildung und Beschreibung der in Nürnbergs Umgebung ohne Kultur wachsenden Pflanzen... Nürnberg (Bei Johann Bernhard Geyer,...) 1817-1821. 3 vols. †. Oct.
**Abbreviated title**: *Nürnb. Fl.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 1817-1821, in fascicles (of *8 pl.*), vols. 1 and 2 completed, vol. 3 only 1 fasc, in all 108 pl. *Copy* at M has: *Vol. 1*: 2 t.p., 4 p. preface, 2 p. index, *49 pl*. with 102 p. text; *vol*.
*2*: t.p., 2 p. index, *49 pl*. with 102 p. text; *vol. 3*: handwritten t.p., *8 pl*. with 16 p. text. — Copies vary apparently; plates and text unnumbered.
*Other issues* (fide NI): 1819-1822, 2 vols., *100 pl*., n.v. "Nürnbergische Flora oder erste Abtheilung der Baierischen Flora".
