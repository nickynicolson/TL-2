### Author

Hall, Charles Albert (1872-1965), English clergyman who held pastorates at Hull, Bristol, Paisley, Southport and London (1896-1935); popular writer on British wild flowers. 
**Abbreviated name**: *C. A. Hall*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
