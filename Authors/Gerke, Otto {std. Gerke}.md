### Author

Gerke, Otto (1873-1946?), German botanist, born 2 Feb 1873 in Oberscheden, Ph.D. 1900 Erlangen, based in Hannover. (*Gerke*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 2: 41 (cites "Tschirsch, Hand. ed. 2, 1: 1053").
