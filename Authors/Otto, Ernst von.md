### Author

Otto, Ernst von (1799-1863), German botanist; landowner at Possendorf nr. Dresden 1826-1856; ultimately at Dresden. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Ernst Otto*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Andrews p. 310; Barnhart 3: 37; BM 3: 1485, 7: 963; CSP 4: 712-713; Jackson p. 185.
- Drechsler, A., S. B. naturw. Ges. Isis. 1864 (1-3): 8-9 (obit., b. 16 Dec 1799, d. 26 Dec 1863).
