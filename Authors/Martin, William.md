### Author

Martin, William (1886-1975), New Zealand botanist and high school teacher, B. Sc. Otago Univ. 1911. 
**Abbreviated name**: *W. Martin*

#### Herbarium and types

Phanerogams at [[Collection WELT|WELT]] (17.000); bryophytes at [[Collection CHR|CHR]] (12.000), lichens at [[Collection CANTY|CANTY]] and [[Collection CHR|CHR]].
