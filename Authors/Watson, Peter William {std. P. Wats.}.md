### Author

Watson, Peter William (1761-1830), British botanist and tradesman of Hull; founded the Hull botanic garden 1812; F.L.S. 1824. (*P. Wats.*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection HUL|HUL]] (destroyed Jun 1943).

### Publications

##### n.16.811. Dendrologia britannica

**Title**
*Dendrologia britannica*, or trees and shrubs that will live in the open air of Britain throughout the year. A work useful to proprietors and possessors of estates, in selecting subjects for planting woods, parks and shrubberies; and also to all persons who cultivate trees and shrubs... London (printed for the author;...) \[1823\]-1825, 2 vols. Qu.
**Abbreviated title**: *Dendrol. brit.*
