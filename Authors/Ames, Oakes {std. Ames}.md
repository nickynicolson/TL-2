### Author

Ames, Oakes (1874-1950), American orchidologist, professor of economic botany at Harvard University (*Ames*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection AMES|AMES]].
*Ref*.: IH 1 (ed. 6): 355, 2: 34.
- Day, Rhodora 3: 68. 1901.
