### Author

Roche, Daniel de la (1743-1813), Swiss (Geneva) botanist; Dr. med. Leiden 1766; practicing physician at Genève 1771-1782, at Paris 1782-1813 with a short period (1792-?) at Lausanne. (*D. Roche*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection G|G]]-DC.
