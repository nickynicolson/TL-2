### Author

Schenck, A. (Adolf?) (*fl*. 1845), German botanist; high school teacher at the teacher's college of Nassau-Dillenburg. 
**Abbreviated name**: *A. Schenck*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Note

Possibly identical with the entomologist Adolf Schenck who also lived at Nassau around 1845-1850. – Adolf Schenck (1857-1936, hence not identical with this author), was a geographer and geologist who explored and collected plants in German S.W. Africa.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1828; CSP 5: 454-455, 8: 849, 11: 302, 12: 654 (for "Ad. Schenck"); Herder p. 188; PR 8150, ed. 1: 9098; Rehder 1: 251, 381.
