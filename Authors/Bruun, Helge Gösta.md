### Author

Bruun, Helge Gösta (1897-x), Swedish cytologist and geneticist, studied at the University of Uppsala, Dr. phil. ib. 1932, lector, head of Strängnäs Läroverk, Strängnäs 1932. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Bruun*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
