### Author

Schuez, \[Georg\] Emil \[Carl Christoph\] (1828-1877), German (Schwabian) physician and botanist; studied at Tübingen Univ. 1846-1851; from 1853 practicing physician at Calw, Nordschwarzwald, Oberschwaben; Dr. med. Tübingen 1858. (*E. Schüz*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection STU|STU]] (Künkele & Seybold 1970).

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 4: 1867 (d. 1877); PR 8455, 8422; Rehder 1: 384.

### Publications

##### n.11.254. Flora des noerdlichen Schwarzwaldes

**Title**
*Flora des noerdlichen Schwarzwaldes*. Inaugural-Abhandlung zur Erlangung der Doctorwürde in der Medicin und Chirurgie, unter dem Präsidium des Dr. Hugo von Mohl,... von G.E.C.C. Schüz, prakt. Arzt zu Calw. Calw (Druck der A. Oelschläger'schen Buchdruckerei) 1858. Oct. (in fours).
**Abbreviated title**: *Fl. noerdl. Schwarzw.*

**Notes**
*Publ*.: 1858 (p. \[iii\]: 19 Apr 1858), p. \[i, iii\], \[1\]-64, chart. *Copies*: B, HBG, HH, NY, U. – A note in the dissertation promises a commercial issue with an added list of pteridophyta and additions to the phanerogams to be issued Easter 1859. This was probably never realized.
