### Author

\* Dieterich, Carl \[Karl\] Friedrich (1734-1805), German jurist and botanist, born 23 Aug 1734 at Erfurt, died also at Erfurt 31 Aug 1805. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Dieter.*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
