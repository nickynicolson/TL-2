### Author

Queva, Charles (*fl*. 1894), French botanist; studied at Lille; from 1902 professor of botany at Dijon. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Queva*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 119; BM 4: 1634; CSP 12: 594, 18: 7; Rehder 5: 697.
