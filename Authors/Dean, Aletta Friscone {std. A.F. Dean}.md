### Author

Dean, Aletta Friscone (fl. 1903-1914), American botanist, at the University of Wisconsin, Ph.B. 1903, Ph.M. 1904, active at Madison, Wisconsin. (*A.F. Dean*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
