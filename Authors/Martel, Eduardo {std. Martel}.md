### Author

Martel, Eduardo (1846-1929), Italian botanist and high school teacher at Torino; ultimately at Finalmarina. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Martel*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Bossert p. 254; CSP 17: 47; De Toni 1: lxxxi; DTS 1: 182, xxi; GR p. 518 (b. 21 Sep 1846, d. 6 Feb 1929), cat. p. 67; Jackson p. 496; Morren ed. 10, 81; Nordstedt p. 22; Saccardo 1: 104.
- Dörfler, J., Botaniker-Adressbuch 99. 1896.
