### Author

Geinitz, Franz Eugen (1854-1925), German geologist, mineralogist and palaeobiologist at Rostock. 
**Abbreviated name**: *F. E. Geinitz*

#### Collections

Mecklenburgisches geologisches Landesmuseum.
