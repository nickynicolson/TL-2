### Author

Watanabe, Kiyohiko (1900-x), Japanese botanist; Dr. phil. Tokyo 1924, college teacher at Hiroshima. (*Watanabe*).

#### Herbarium and types

Material collected by "K. Watanabe", probably not Kiyohiko W., is reported from [[Collection BAS|BAS]], [[Collection GH|GH]], [[Collection K|K]], [[Collection TI|TI]]. – Kano Watanabe at Tokyo collected plants in Japan 1886-1897 and distributed these to European and American institutions. – *Letters *at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 462; Hirsch p. 316; MW suppl. p. 392.
