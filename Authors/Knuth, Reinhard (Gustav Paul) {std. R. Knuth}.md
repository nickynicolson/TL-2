### Author

Knuth, Reinhard \[Gustav Paul\] (1874-1957), German botanist; pupil of Engler; Dr. phil. Berlin 1902; high school teacher at Breslau 1900-1905; at Berlin 1905-1937; research associate at the Botanischem Museum, Berlin-Dahlem. (*R. Knuth*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection B|B]] (26.000); destroyed 1/2 March 1943 together with his library and manuscripts among which the finished monograph of the Elaeocarpaceae for the *Pflanzenreich*.
*Ref*.: Melchior, H., Taxon 6: 185-186. 1957.
