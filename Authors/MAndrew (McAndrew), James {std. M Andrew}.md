### Author

M'Andrew \[McAndrew\], James (1836-1917), British school-teacher and botanist. (*M' Andrew*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection E|E]] (mosses, lichens); further material at [[Collection DFS|DFS]], [[Collection MANCH|MANCH]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]].
