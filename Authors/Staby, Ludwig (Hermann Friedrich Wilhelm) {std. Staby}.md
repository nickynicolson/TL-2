### Author

Staby, Ludwig \[Hermann Friedrich Wilhelm\] (1861-?), German (Westphalian/Prussian) botanist; studied natural sciences in Jena, 1881-1883, and Berlin 1883-1885; student of Schwendener. (*Staby*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
