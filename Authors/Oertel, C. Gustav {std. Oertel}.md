### Author

Oertel, C. Gustav (1833-1908), German botanist and high school teacher at Gehofen (nr. Artern). (*Oertel*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection HAL|HAL]] (Werner 1955); contributed to Sydow, *Mycotheca germanica*; further material at [[Collection B|B]], [[Collection E|E]], [[Collection IBF|IBF]], [[Collection OXF|OXF]] and [[Collection WAG|WAG]].
