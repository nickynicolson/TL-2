### Author

\* De Moor, V.P.G. (1827-1895), Belgian government veterinarian living at Alost (E. Flandria), studying especially the Belgian grasses, born at Alost 25 Jul 1827, died ib. 25 Jan 1895. 
**Abbreviated name**: *De Moor*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.
