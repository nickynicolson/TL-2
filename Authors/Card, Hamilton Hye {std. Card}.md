### Author

Card, Hamilton Hye (1877-1953), American botanist and teacher, AB at University of Illinois, Dr. phil. 1931 at Washington University, later school teacher in Fillmore, Illinois. (*Card*).

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
