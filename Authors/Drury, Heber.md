### Author

Drury, Heber (1819-1872), British colonel in the Madras Light Infantry, student of the flora of India. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Drury*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BB p. 96; CSP 2: 347; Jackson p. 540; PR 2421-2422.
