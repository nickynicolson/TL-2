### Author

Nicolai, Ernst August (1800-1874), German botanist and practicing physician at Arnstadt (nr. Erfurt, Thüringen) 
**Abbreviated name**: *Nicolai*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography
