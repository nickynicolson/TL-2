### Author

Tucker, Ethelyn \[Daliaette\] Maria (1871-?), American botanical librarian; assistant Forbes Library, Northampton, Mass. 1897; assistant Harvard College 1897-1904; assistant Arnold Arboretum library 1904-1918; librarian 1918-1933. 
**Abbreviated name**: *E.M. Tucker*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

Barnhart 3: 406; Tucker 3: 314.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Index Amer. bot. lit. 1886-1966, 4: 388. 1969.
- Howes, D., ed., Amer. women 3: 920. 1939 (published *Plants and the men whose name they commemorate* 1938; in 1939 still working at Arnold Arb.).
- Lawrence, A., Who's who N. Amer. authors 960. s.d.
