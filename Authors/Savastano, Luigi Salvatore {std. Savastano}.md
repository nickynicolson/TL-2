### Author

Savastano, Luigi Salvatore (1853- ?), Italian botanist and phytopathologist; assistant at the botanical garden of the R. Scuola superiore d'Agricoltura Portici, nr Napoli. 
**Abbreviated name**: *Savastano*

#### Herbarium and types

Unknown.

#### Bibliography and biography

BM 8: 1142; Bossert p. 350 (b. 4 Feb 1853); CSP 18: 464; Herder p. 376; Kelly p. 206; Kew 4: 605; LS 4750, 23748-23763a, 38810-38822, 41703; Morren ed. 10, p. 87; PH 59; Rehder 1: 430, 5: 761; Saccardo 1: 147; Stevenson p. 1257; Tucker 1: 625, 3: 269.
