### Author

Walz., Jacob Jacoblevic (1840-1904), Russian botanist; studied in Kiev and Berlin (with P.F.A. Ascherson); professor of botany in Odessa. (*J. Walz*).

#### Herbarium and types

[[Collection MW|MW]] (200). – *Letters* at [[Collection G|G]].

#### Bibliography and biography

De Toni 1: cxxviii-cxxix; Frank 3 (Anh.): 110; Herder p. 255; LS 28283-28284; Morren ed. 2, p. 30; PR 9989; TR 1546-1557.

#### Biofile

- Anon., Acta Horti Univ. Jurjev. 5: 272-273. 1904 (obit. not.); Nuov. Notarisia 15: 127. 1904 (d.); Science ser. 2. 21: 158. 1905 (d.).
- Bourdeille de Montrésor, Bull. Soc. Imp. Natural. Moscou ser. 2. 7: 483-484. 1894 (bibl.).

#### Eponymy

The eponymy of *Walzia* Sorokin (1875) could not be determined.
