**Notes**: Updated Nov 2017. The POZ Herbarium is composed of various collections, mainly from Poland, but also from different regions of Europe – altogether, about 190,000 of specimens. Furthermore, the Herbarium includes some special collections, including the collections made by J. Paczoski (from the Balkan Peninsula – 9,000 sheets), G. A. Ritschl (from the Silesia and Western Poland – 5,000 sheets) and Walter (from North America – 1,200 sheets). The whole collection contains over 240 nomenclatural types of different ranks. The collected specimens can be used for taxonomic and comparative purposes, biometric and phytogeographical studies and as reference material for rare and protected species localities.
