**Notes**: The main activities at TAN Herbarium are the following
- Conservation of herbarium 350 000 mounted specimens divided into 280 families, 1 885 genera and 8 745 species (2019)
- Inventory and collection of herbarium specimens
- Receipt of collected botanical samples from plant collectors
- Mandated by the Malagasy Government to receive the duplicate of specimens collected by foreign and national researchers before any specimens exportation.
- Digitizing and data-basing by using Botanical Research And Herbarium Management System (BRAHMS) software.
- Support tools for foreign and national researchers and high school students. 
- Assessment, review of conservation status of malagasy plants.
- Partnership with national institutions (Herbier TEF, DBEV and ENS) and international organizations (MBG, RBG Kew, CAS, HBV and IRD).
- Supervision of trainee students from public and private universities (on average 60 per year).
