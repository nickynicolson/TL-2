**Date founded**: 1940

**Taxonomic coverage**: Marine algae, especially Rhodophyta; flowering plants, mosses and lichens

**Geographic coverage**: Ireland

**Important collectors**: M. De Valéra, M. Guiry

**Notes**: Updated Aug 2013.  M. Guiry retired in 2009, and the algal materials (those of Guiry and Mairin De Valera)  are now in the Ryan Institute, National University of Ireland Galway. The small flowering plant herbarium is still at the Botany & Plant Science discipline of the School of Natural Sciences. The code  "GALW" applies to both collections. The algal herbarium has 15,500 numbered specimens; the flowering plants, mosses and lichen herbarium is less than 2,000 specimens.
