**Notes**: Updated Nov 2017.  Herbarium is divided into (i) Specimens from outside Jamaica (mainly Caribbean), (ii)Monocots from Jamaica, (iii)Dicots from Jamaica ,(iv)Gymnosperms,Ferns and Algae from Jamaica.(v)Bryophytes and Lichen from Jamaica.  Specimens are in folders arranged alphabetically, in family, genus and species.  About 95% of cabinets are modern steel type with the rest wooden.  The relatively small virtual component (see Website URL above) was the work of Dr. Philip Rose for his doctoral thesis.
